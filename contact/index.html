Common Questions

How do I book a character for my party?

You can book directly through our online booking form, call us at 856-200-8156, or fill out the contact form above. We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for best availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. See our full cancellation policy for details.

Do you serve my area?

We serve 15 cities across the United States. Check our locations page to find your nearest service area. If you don't see your city listed, contact us - we may still be able to help!

How much does it cost to book a character?

Pricing starts at $199 for the first hour and varies based on character, location, and event details. Contact us for a personalized quote.
