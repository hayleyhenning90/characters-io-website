About Our Woody

The Ultimate Toy Story Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Woody from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings Andy's favorite cowboy to life. Our Woody entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting his loyal personality, cowboy swagger, and heartfelt charm.

Every detail matters when creating authentic Toy Story party entertainment. Our Woody costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic yellow plaid shirt, cow-print vest, brown cowboy hat, and sheriff badge that children recognize and love. The costume photographs wonderfully, ensuring your party pictures capture the magic of Toy Story.

What sets our Woody apart is his genuine connection with kids. Children are captivated as he shares stories about his adventures with Buzz Lightyear, teaches them cowboy skills, and reminds everyone that "You've got a friend in me." His friendly personality and warm interactions make every child feel like part of Andy's room.

Perfect for Toy Story-Themed Birthday Parties

A Toy Story birthday party with Woody is a beloved theme for children ages 2-10. Our Woody character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a backyard—into Andy's world of imagination. He arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special deputy, and spends the entire visit creating cowboy adventures.

The "You've Got a Friend in Me" singalong is always a highlight. Woody gathers all the children together to sing the iconic theme song, creating a heartfelt moment that parents love to capture on video. Then he leads the group in cowboy games, lasso tricks, and an exciting roundup adventure that children will talk about for weeks.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Woody

How much does it cost to hire Woody for a birthday party?

Woody party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Woody do during the "You've Got a Friend in Me" singalong?

The singalong is the heartwarming highlight of every Woody appearance! He gathers all the children together and leads them in singing the beloved Toy Story theme song. Kids love singing along with their favorite cowboy, and parents treasure capturing this special moment on video. It's a beautiful reminder of the friendship and loyalty that makes Toy Story so special.

What ages is Woody best for?

Our Woody character is perfect for children ages 2-10, with the sweet spot being 3-8 year olds who love Toy Story. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to hear about Woody's adventures. The cowboy fun appeals to anyone who loves Toy Story!

How far in advance should I book Woody for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Woody is a popular character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during birthday season (spring and summer). For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Woody with Jessie or Buzz Lightyear?

Absolutely! Booking Woody with Jessie is a magical combination that brings the Toy Story world to life. Together they recreate the beloved cowboy adventures, lead roundup games, and create twice the excitement. We also offer Woody paired with Buzz Lightyear for the ultimate "To infinity and beyond!" party experience, or you can book all three for a complete Toy Story celebration.

What does Woody do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Woody visit includes: a cowboy entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child (who becomes an honorary deputy), cowboy games like stick horse races and boot toss, lasso trick demonstrations and lessons, the "You've Got a Friend in Me" singalong, a roundup adventure, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, storytime about Andy's room, and additional activities.

Is your Woody performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Woody come for parties?

Woody can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. Outdoor settings are especially fun for cowboy-themed parties! We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
