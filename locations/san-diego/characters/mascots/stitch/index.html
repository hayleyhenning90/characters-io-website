Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Stitch

How much does it cost to hire Stitch for a birthday party?

Stitch party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Stitch do at parties?

Our Stitch character leads fun activities inspired by the beloved Disney film! Favorites include Ohana dance parties, alien-themed games, Hawaiian luau fun, and mischief making that gets everyone laughing. Kids love experiencing their favorite character's playful energy in real life!

What ages is Stitch best for?

Our Stitch character is perfect for children ages 2-10. The character appeals to a wide age range thanks to the timeless Disney film that continues to delight new generations. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging children of all ages, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Stitch for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Stitch is a popular Disney character, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Stitch and Lilo together?

Absolutely! Booking Stitch and Lilo together creates the ultimate Lilo & Stitch party experience. When you hire both characters, they interact as the beloved duo from the film, lead activities together, and create twice the ohana fun. The Lilo add-on is discounted when booked with Stitch.

What does Stitch do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Stitch visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Ohana dance party, alien games, Hawaiian luau activities, plenty of hugs and mischief, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Stitch performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Stitch come for parties?

Stitch can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
