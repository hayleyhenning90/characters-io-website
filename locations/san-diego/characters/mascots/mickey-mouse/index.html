Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Mickey Mouse

How much does it cost to hire Mickey Mouse for a birthday party?

Mickey Mouse party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Mickey Mouse do at parties?

Our Mickey Mouse character leads the iconic Hot Dog dance party from Mickey Mouse Clubhouse, plays clubhouse-inspired games, leads character meet and greets, and creates magical photo opportunities. Kids love experiencing the Disney magic in real life!

What ages is Mickey Mouse best for?

Our Mickey Mouse character is perfect for children ages 1-7, with the sweet spot being toddlers and preschoolers who are big fans of Mickey Mouse Clubhouse and classic Disney. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging very young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Mickey Mouse for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Mickey Mouse is a timeless classic and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse together?

Absolutely! Booking Mickey and Minnie together is our most popular Disney duo package. When you hire both characters, they interact as the beloved couple from the show, lead games together, and create twice the Disney magic. The Minnie add-on is discounted when booked with Mickey.

What does Mickey Mouse do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Mickey Mouse visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Hot Dog dance party, clubhouse games, character meet and greet with all guests, plenty of hugs and photos, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Mickey Mouse performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Mickey Mouse come for parties?

Mickey Mouse can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
