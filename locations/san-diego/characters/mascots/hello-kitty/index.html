Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Hello Kitty

How much does it cost to hire Hello Kitty for a birthday party?

Hello Kitty party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Hello Kitty do at parties?

Our Hello Kitty character leads adorable kawaii-themed activities! Favorites include hosting a tea party, friendship games, bow decorating crafts, and kawaii dance parties. Kids love experiencing the sweet world of Sanrio in real life!

What ages is Hello Kitty best for?

Our Hello Kitty character is perfect for children ages 2-8, with the sweet spot being preschoolers and early elementary kids who love cute characters. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging toddlers and young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Hello Kitty for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Hello Kitty is one of our most beloved mascot characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Hello Kitty with My Melody?

Absolutely! Booking both Sanrio friends together is a popular duo package. When you hire Hello Kitty and My Melody together, they interact as best friends, lead activities together, and create twice the kawaii fun. The My Melody add-on is discounted when booked with Hello Kitty.

What does Hello Kitty do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Hello Kitty visit includes: an adorable entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, kawaii-themed activities like tea party and friendship games, a dance party, plenty of hugs and cuddles, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add bow decorating crafts and more activities.

Is your Hello Kitty performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Hello Kitty come for parties?

Hello Kitty can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
