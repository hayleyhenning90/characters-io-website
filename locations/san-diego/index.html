San Diego FAQ

San Diego Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in San Diego?

Character party packages in San Diego start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within San Diego County. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in San Diego do you serve?

We serve all of San Diego County including La Jolla, Carlsbad, Coronado, Encinitas, Chula Vista, Del Mar, Oceanside, Escondido, El Cajon, Poway, Rancho Santa Fe, Pacific Beach, and all surrounding areas.

How far in advance should I book in San Diego?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for San Diego parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

What do the characters do at San Diego parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Face painting is included; balloons are available for an additional fee.

Do you perform at beaches and parks in San Diego?

We perform at homes, beaches, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout San Diego. Beach and park parties are very popular - we love San Diego's beautiful outdoor venues!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
