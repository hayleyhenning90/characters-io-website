Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Superman

How much does it cost to hire Superman for a birthday party?

Superman party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Superman do at the party?

Our Superman leads hero training sessions where kids learn about courage and kindness. He demonstrates flying poses, runs super strength challenges, shares Krypton story time, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song, and makes the birthday child feel like a true hero. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

What ages is Superman best for?

Our Superman character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love superheroes. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little in awe to older kids who want to learn hero moves. We've even done Superman appearances for teens and adults who are lifelong fans!

How far in advance should I book Superman for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Superman is one of our most popular superhero characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Superman with other superheroes?

Absolutely! Booking Superman with Batman is a popular combo for superhero parties—the World's Finest team-up! When you hire multiple heroes, they interact and create an epic team-up experience for the kids. We also offer Superman paired with Wonder Woman or Supergirl for a Super Family experience. The second character is discounted when booked together.

What does a typical Superman visit include?

A typical 1-hour Superman visit includes: a dramatic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, hero training session, flying pose practice, super strength challenges, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add Krypton story time, more games, and additional activities.

Is your Superman performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Superman come for parties?

Superman can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
