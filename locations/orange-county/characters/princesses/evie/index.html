Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Evie

How much does it cost to hire Evie for a birthday party?

Evie party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Evie do at parties?

Our Evie leads exciting activities including fashion design sessions where kids sketch outfits and explore style, mirror mirror games celebrating inner beauty, glamorous makeover inspiration, VK dance parties with Descendants choreography, and photo opportunities. She brings her signature fashionista flair to create an atmosphere of creativity and self-expression.

What ages is Evie best for?

Our Evie character is perfect for children ages 6-12, with the sweet spot being 7-11 year olds who love the Descendants movies, fashion, and style. Our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who adore Evie's blue hair to tweens who appreciate her fashion expertise and VK attitude.

How far in advance should I book Evie for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Evie is very popular, especially for Descendants-themed parties, and weekend dates fill up quickly during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Evie with Mal or other Descendants characters?

Absolutely! Booking Evie and Mal together is our most popular Descendants duo package. When you hire both, they interact as best friends from the Isle of the Lost, creating twice the magic and the ultimate VK celebration. We also offer Evie paired with Uma or other Descendants characters for the complete villain kid experience.

Does Evie do fashion design activities?

Yes! Fashion is Evie's specialty and passion. She leads creative fashion design activities where kids can sketch outfits, learn about style elements, and discover their own creative flair. Evie shares tips about color, accessories, and self-expression—it's one of the most popular and memorable activities at every Evie party!

Is your Evie performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Evie come for parties?

Evie can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
