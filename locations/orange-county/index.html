OC FAQ

Orange County Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Orange County?

Character party packages in Orange County start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within OC. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Orange County do you serve?

We serve all of Orange County including Anaheim, Irvine, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Costa Mesa, Laguna Beach, Fullerton, Orange, Mission Viejo, Lake Forest, Tustin, Yorba Linda, and all surrounding areas.

Can you come to Disneyland-area hotels in Anaheim?

Yes! We regularly perform at hotels near Disneyland in Anaheim for birthday parties and special celebrations. Our characters are completely independent and not affiliated with Disney parks.

How far in advance should I book in Orange County?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Orange County parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

Do you serve beach parties in OC?

Our characters can attend beach parties in Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Laguna Beach, and other OC beaches. Moana and our other characters are perfect for beachside celebrations!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
