IE FAQ

Inland Empire Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in the Inland Empire?

Character party packages in the Inland Empire start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within the IE. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in the Inland Empire do you serve?

We serve all of Riverside and San Bernardino counties including Riverside, San Bernardino, Ontario, Rancho Cucamonga, Fontana, Corona, Moreno Valley, Temecula, Murrieta, Redlands, Chino Hills, Upland, and all surrounding areas.

Do you serve the Temecula wine country area?

Yes! We regularly perform at parties in Temecula, Murrieta, and the surrounding wine country area. Our characters can come to homes, parks, wineries with event spaces, and party venues throughout southwest Riverside County.

How far in advance should I book in the Inland Empire?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for IE parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

Do you perform at parks in the Inland Empire?

Yes! We perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout the IE. Fairmount Park in Riverside, Victoria Gardens in Rancho Cucamonga, and other IE parks are popular choices.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
