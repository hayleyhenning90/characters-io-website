About Our Belle

The Ultimate Beauty and the Beast Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Belle from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the bookworm princess to life. Our Belle entertainers are actresses who have studied Beauty and the Beast extensively, perfecting Belle's voice, mannerisms, and her curious, kind-hearted nature.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Belle costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic yellow ball gown from the film, complete with hand-sewn details, flowing skirts, and the golden elegance that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Belle apart is her love of storytelling. Belle brings her passion for books and adventure to every party, leading enchanting story time sessions that captivate children and inspire a love of reading. She engages children with book-themed games, fairy tale activities, and the kind of imaginative play that Belle herself would adore.

Our Belle character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a restaurant—into an enchanted castle. Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Belle

How much does it cost to hire Belle for a birthday party?

Belle party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does Belle do story time at parties?

Absolutely! Story time is one of Belle's signature activities. Our Belle performer brings her love of books to every party, reading beloved fairy tales and stories to captivated children. She makes story time interactive and engaging, inspiring a love of reading just like the bookworm princess herself.

What ages is Belle best for?

Our Belle character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love princess stories and fairy tales. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to chat about their favorite books. Belle's timeless appeal works for all ages!

How far in advance should I book Belle for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Belle is a beloved princess character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can Belle host a tea party at my event?

Yes! A tea party is one of Belle's most popular activities. Inspired by Mrs. Potts and Chip from Beauty and the Beast, Belle hosts an elegant tea party experience, teaching children proper tea etiquette while sharing stories of her enchanted castle adventures. It's perfect for Beauty and the Beast themed parties!

What does Belle do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Belle visit includes: a grand entrance in her iconic yellow ball gown, special greeting for the birthday child, enchanting story time sessions, book-themed games and activities, princess etiquette lessons, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add tea party activities, more games, and additional storytime.

Is your Belle performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Belle come for parties?

Belle can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
