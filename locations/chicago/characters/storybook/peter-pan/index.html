About Our Peter Pan

The Ultimate Neverland Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Peter Pan from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings Neverland's eternal boy hero to life. Our Peter Pan entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting his mischievous charm, adventurous spirit, and heroic bravery.

Every detail matters when creating an authentic Neverland party experience. Our Peter Pan costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic green tunic with jagged leaf edges, brown belt, and the signature feathered cap that children recognize and love. Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Peter Pan

How much does it cost to hire Peter Pan for a birthday party?

Peter Pan party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Peter Pan do during sword fighting lessons?

Sword fighting lessons are one of the most popular activities! Peter Pan demonstrates his signature moves and teaches children safe swashbuckling techniques using foam swords. He shows them how to parry, thrust, and defend like a true Neverland hero. The children practice their moves and then get to "battle" pirates in an exciting game that's both fun and safe for all ages.

What ages is Peter Pan best for?

Our Peter Pan character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love adventure and imaginative play. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to learn advanced sword fighting moves. The Neverland adventure appeals to anyone who believes in magic!

How far in advance should I book Peter Pan for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Peter Pan is a popular character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Peter Pan with Tinkerbell?

Absolutely! Booking Peter Pan with Tinkerbell is a magical combination that brings Neverland to life. Together they recreate the beloved adventures, lead flying lessons with pixie dust, and create twice the enchantment. We also offer Peter Pan paired with Captain Hook for an exciting pirate battle experience that kids absolutely love.

What does Peter Pan do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Peter Pan visit includes: a heroic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child as the newest Lost Boy, exciting sword fighting lessons, flying lessons teaching children the secrets of faith, trust, and pixie dust, pirate battle games and Lost Boys adventures, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake celebration. For 2-hour packages, we add treasure hunts, more games, and a storytime about Neverland adventures.

Is your Peter Pan performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Peter Pan come for parties?

Peter Pan can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. Outdoor settings with space for sword fighting and adventure games are especially fun! We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
