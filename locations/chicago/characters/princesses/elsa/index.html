About Our Elsa

The Ultimate Frozen Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Elsa from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the Snow Queen to life. Our Elsa entertainers are actresses and singers who have studied the Frozen films extensively, perfecting Elsa's voice, mannerisms, and regal bearing.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Elsa costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic ice dress from Frozen, complete with hand-sewn details, a flowing cape, and the signature snowflake patterns that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Elsa apart is the live singing. Many princess performers simply play recorded music, but our Elsa actually sings "Let It Go," "Do You Want to Build a Snowman," "Into the Unknown," and other Frozen favorites in person. Children's faces light up when they hear their favorite songs performed live by the Snow Queen herself.

Perfect for Frozen-Themed Birthday Parties

A Frozen birthday party with Elsa is the most requested princess party theme for girls ages 3-8. Our Elsa character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a restaurant—into the kingdom of Arendelle. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special princess, and spends the entire visit creating magical moments.

The coronation ceremony is always a highlight. Elsa crowns the birthday child as an honorary Princess of Arendelle, complete with a tiara and royal proclamation. Then she leads the group in Frozen-themed games like freeze dance (where everyone must freeze when the music stops), snowball toss, and the "Do You Want to Build a Snowman" activity.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Elsa

How much does it cost to hire Elsa for a birthday party?

Elsa party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Elsa actually sing "Let It Go" live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Elsa entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "Let It Go," "Do You Want to Build a Snowman," "Into the Unknown," and other Frozen favorites performed in person by the Snow Queen herself. It's always a highlight of the party!

What ages is Elsa best for?

Our Elsa character is perfect for children ages 2-10, with the sweet spot being 3-7 year olds who are deeply into Frozen. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to chat about Arendelle. We've even done Elsa appearances for teens and adults who grew up with Frozen!

How far in advance should I book Elsa for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Elsa is our most popular princess character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Elsa and Anna together?

Absolutely! Booking both Frozen sisters is our most popular princess duo package. When you hire Elsa and Anna together, they interact as the loving sisters from the films, perform songs together, and create twice the magic. The Anna add-on is discounted when booked with Elsa. We also offer Elsa paired with Rapunzel, Cinderella, or other princesses for a "princess party" theme.

What does Elsa do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Elsa visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, live singing performances, a coronation ceremony where she crowns the birthday child as a Princess of Arendelle, Frozen-themed games and activities, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, a storytime, and additional activities.

Is your Elsa performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Elsa come for parties?

Elsa can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
