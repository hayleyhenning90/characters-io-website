About Our Stormtrooper

The Ultimate Star Wars Birthday Party Experience

When you hire a Stormtrooper from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the Galactic Empire to life. Our Stormtrooper entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting the Imperial soldier's rigid stance, commanding presence, and military bearing.

Every detail matters when creating authentic Star Wars party entertainment. Our Stormtrooper armor is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic white plastoid suit, complete with detailed helmet, chest plate, and all the gear that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look like scenes from a galaxy far, far away.

What sets our Stormtrooper apart is the immersive experience. Our performer stays in character the entire time, moving and interacting with children as a real Imperial soldier would. Kids' faces light up when they meet this iconic character in person and get to train alongside them.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring a Stormtrooper

How much does it cost to hire a Stormtrooper for a birthday party?

Stormtrooper party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does a Stormtrooper do at the party?

Our Stormtrooper leads Imperial training sessions where kids learn soldier formations and stances. They run blaster target practice games, conduct patrol missions around the party venue, take photos with all guests, help with the birthday song, and make the birthday child feel like a true recruit of the Empire. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

What ages is a Stormtrooper best for?

Our Stormtrooper character is perfect for children ages 4-14, with the sweet spot being 6-12 year olds who love Star Wars. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little in awe to teens who appreciate the authentic costume. We've even done Stormtrooper appearances for adults who are lifelong Star Wars fans!

How far in advance should I book a Stormtrooper for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Stormtrooper is one of our most popular Star Wars characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book a Stormtrooper with other Star Wars characters?

Absolutely! Booking Stormtrooper with Darth Vader is a popular combo for Star Wars parties. When you hire multiple characters, they interact and create an epic galactic experience for the kids. We also offer Stormtrooper paired with Jedi or Rey for a light side vs. dark side adventure. The second character is discounted when booked together.

What does a typical Stormtrooper visit include?

A typical 1-hour Stormtrooper visit includes: a dramatic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, Imperial training session, blaster target practice, patrol games, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, a galactic mission story, and additional activities.

Is your Stormtrooper performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can a Stormtrooper come for parties?

Stormtrooper can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
