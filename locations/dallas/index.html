DFW FAQ

Dallas Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Dallas?

Character party packages in Dallas start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within the DFW metroplex. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Dallas do you serve?

We serve all of Dallas-Fort Worth including Plano, Frisco, Fort Worth, Arlington, McKinney, Allen, Irving, Carrollton, Richardson, Garland, Southlake, Flower Mound, Grapevine, and all surrounding areas.

How far in advance should I book in Dallas?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for DFW parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

What do the characters do at DFW parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Face painting is included; balloons are available for an additional fee.

Do you perform at venues or just homes in Dallas?

We perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout Dallas-Fort Worth. We can come to any venue that's accessible - Klyde Warren Park, AT&T Stadium area, or your backyard!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
