Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Mario

How much does it cost to hire Mario for a birthday party?

Mario party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What games does Mario play at parties?

Our Mario character leads exciting games inspired by the video game! Favorites include Mushroom Kingdom adventures, coin collecting challenges, obstacle courses, and dance parties. Kids love experiencing their favorite Mario adventures in real life!

What ages is Mario best for?

Our Mario character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being kids who love video games and adventure. Our performer is skilled at engaging children of all ages, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Mario for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Mario is a popular video game character, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book other video game characters with Mario?

Absolutely! Mario pairs great with Luigi, Princess Peach, and even Sonic for an epic video game crossover party. When you book multiple characters together, they interact and create an even more exciting party atmosphere. Contact us for multi-character package pricing.

What does Mario do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Mario visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Mario-themed games like coin collecting and obstacle courses, a dance party, plenty of high-fives and photos with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Mario performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Mario come for parties?

Mario can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
