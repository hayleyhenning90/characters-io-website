Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Baby Shark

How much does it cost to hire Baby Shark for a birthday party?

Baby Shark party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like bubble machines or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Baby Shark do at parties?

Our Baby Shark character leads the world-famous Baby Shark dance, ocean-themed games, bubble fun, and shark family singalongs! Kids love doing the iconic hand movements and singing doo doo doo doo doo doo with their favorite character.

What ages is Baby Shark best for?

Our Baby Shark character is perfect for children ages 1-5, with the sweet spot being toddlers who are devoted fans of the viral song. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging babies and toddlers, including those who might be a bit shy at first with gentle, patient interaction.

How far in advance should I book Baby Shark for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Baby Shark is extremely popular for toddler birthday parties, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book multiple characters with Baby Shark?

Absolutely! Baby Shark pairs wonderfully with other beloved kids characters. Popular combinations include Baby Shark with Cocomelon JJ, Bluey, or Paw Patrol characters. Adding a second character creates even more excitement and entertainment for the little ones.

What does a typical Baby Shark party visit include?

A typical 1-hour Baby Shark visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, the famous Baby Shark dance, ocean games, bubble fun, shark family singalong, plenty of hugs and cuddles, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more songs and activities.

Is your Baby Shark performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Baby Shark come for parties?

Baby Shark can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, daycare, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
