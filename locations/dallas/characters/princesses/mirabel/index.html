About Our Mirabel

The Ultimate Encanto Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Mirabel from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the heart of the Madrigal family to life. Our Mirabel entertainers are actresses who have studied Encanto extensively, perfecting Mirabel's enthusiastic spirit, quirky humor, and heartfelt determination.

Every detail matters when creating authentic character party entertainment. Our Mirabel costume is a premium recreation of her iconic look—the beautifully embroidered white blouse with colorful butterfly and flower designs, the flowing teal skirt with traditional Colombian patterns, and of course, her signature round glasses. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures capture the magic of Encanto.

What sets our Mirabel apart is the interactive experience. Our performers lead engaging singalongs of "We Don't Talk About Bruno," "Waiting on a Miracle," and other beloved Encanto songs. Children's faces light up when they get to sing along with Mirabel herself about the mysterious Bruno and the magical Madrigal family.

Perfect for Encanto-Themed Birthday Parties

An Encanto birthday party with Mirabel has become one of the most requested character themes for children ages 3-10. Our Mirabel character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a restaurant—into the magical world of the Madrigals. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special member of the familia, and spends the entire visit creating magical moments.

The casita adventure is always a highlight. Mirabel leads the children through interactive games inspired by the magical house, teaching them about the special gifts of each family member while celebrating what makes everyone unique. The butterfly magic activities bring wonder and enchantment, just like the butterflies that guide Mirabel on her journey to save the miracle.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Mirabel

How much does it cost to hire Mirabel for a birthday party?

Mirabel party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Mirabel sing "We Don't Talk About Bruno"?

Yes! Our Mirabel performers lead an interactive singalong of "We Don't Talk About Bruno," "Waiting on a Miracle," "The Family Madrigal," and other Encanto favorites. Children love singing along with Mirabel herself, making it one of the highlights of every Encanto party!

What ages is Mirabel best for?

Our Mirabel character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who are enchanted by Encanto. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to talk about the Madrigal family's gifts. Mirabel's relatable character appeals to a wide age range!

How far in advance should I book Mirabel for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Mirabel has become one of our most popular characters since Encanto's release, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Mirabel and Isabela together?

Absolutely! Booking both Encanto sisters is a wonderful option for fans of the Madrigal family. When you hire Mirabel and Isabela together, they interact as the loving sisters from the film, creating twice the magic. The Isabela add-on is discounted when booked with Mirabel. We also offer Mirabel paired with Moana or Rapunzel for a diverse princess party experience.

What does Mirabel do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Mirabel visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, "We Don't Talk About Bruno" singalong, casita adventure games and activities, butterfly magic moments, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, storytime about the Madrigal family, and additional activities.

Is your Mirabel performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Mirabel come for parties?

Mirabel can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
