About Our Mulan

The Ultimate Warrior Princess Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Mulan from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the legendary warrior princess to life. Our Mulan entertainers are actresses and performers who have studied the film extensively, perfecting Mulan's brave spirit, graceful movements, and inspiring presence.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Mulan costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of her iconic pink and purple matchmaker dress, complete with traditional Chinese styling, beautiful fan props, and the signature hair accessories that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Mulan apart is her empowering message. Mulan teaches children that true courage comes from within, that being yourself is the greatest honor, and that anyone can be a hero. Our performers bring these themes to life through interactive activities, storytelling, and the timeless song "Reflection."

Perfect for Empowering Birthday Celebrations

A Mulan birthday party is perfect for children who love adventure, bravery, and breaking boundaries. Our Mulan character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a restaurant—into ancient China. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a fellow warrior, and spends the entire visit creating empowering moments.

The honor ceremony is always a highlight. Mulan recognizes the birthday child for their bravery and inner strength, presenting them with a special honor. Then she leads the group in warrior training, teaching fun martial arts-inspired moves, followed by graceful fan dancing that celebrates Chinese culture and tradition.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Mulan

How much does it cost to hire Mulan for a birthday party?

Mulan party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Mulan actually sing "Reflection" live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Mulan entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "Reflection," "Honor to Us All," and other favorites performed in person by the warrior princess herself. It's always a powerful highlight of the party!

What ages is Mulan best for?

Our Mulan character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love adventure and empowering stories. Mulan appeals to children who want to be brave and strong, regardless of gender. Our performers are skilled at engaging all ages with age-appropriate warrior training and activities.

How far in advance should I book Mulan for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and around Lunar New Year celebrations. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Mulan with other adventurous princesses?

Absolutely! Mulan pairs wonderfully with other strong, adventurous princesses. Popular pairings include Mulan with Moana for an adventure theme, Mulan with Jasmine for an exotic princess duo, or Mulan with Pocahontas for a nature warrior celebration. We also offer Mulan paired with any other princess for a "princess party" theme.

What does Mulan do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Mulan visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, live singing performances, warrior training with fun martial arts-inspired moves, traditional fan dancing, an honor ceremony recognizing the birthday child's bravery, reflection meditation, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities, storytelling, and games.

Is your Mulan performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Mulan come for parties?

Mulan can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
