About Our Anna

The Ultimate Frozen Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Anna from Characters.io, you're booking a performer who embodies everything children love about the spirited Princess of Arendelle—her warmth, her adventurous spirit, her boundless optimism, and her loving heart. Our Anna entertainers are actresses and singers who capture Anna's playful energy and genuine kindness.

Anna is the heart of Frozen, and our performers bring that warmth to every party. Children connect instantly with Anna's approachable, fun-loving personality. She's the princess who isn't afraid to be silly, who loves chocolate almost as much as her sister, and who believes that love is the most powerful force in the world.

Our Anna costume is a stunning recreation of her signature Norwegian-inspired dress with the beautiful rosemaling embroidery, magenta cape, and iconic strawberry-blonde braids. Every detail is crafted to bring the beloved character to life, from the crocus patterns to the velvet bodice.

Perfect for Frozen-Themed Birthday Parties

Anna brings a special kind of magic to birthday parties—the magic of friendship, adventure, and unconditional love. She arrives ready to make your child feel like the most special person in the room, sharing stories of Arendelle and leading everyone on imaginative adventures.

One of the highlights is when Anna sings "Do You Want to Build a Snowman," often bringing both kids and parents to happy tears. She also performs "For the First Time in Forever" and can duet "Love Is an Open Door" with the birthday child. Her genuine enthusiasm makes every moment feel like an adventure.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Anna

How much does it cost to hire Anna for a birthday party?

Anna party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Anna actually sing "Do You Want to Build a Snowman" live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Anna entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "Do You Want to Build a Snowman," "For the First Time in Forever," "Love Is an Open Door," and other Frozen favorites performed in person. It's always a highlight of the party!

What ages is Anna best for?

Our Anna character is perfect for children ages 2-10, with the sweet spot being 3-7 year olds who love Frozen. Anna's warm, playful personality makes her especially great with younger children who might be a bit shy—her approachable nature helps them open up quickly. She's also wonderful for older kids who appreciate her adventurous spirit!

How far in advance should I book Anna for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Anna is one of our most beloved princess characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Anna and Elsa together?

Absolutely! Booking both Frozen sisters is our most popular princess duo package. When you hire Anna and Elsa together, they interact as the loving sisters from the films, perform songs together, and create twice the magic. The Elsa add-on is discounted when booked with Anna. Watching them reunite and sing together is always an emotional highlight for guests of all ages!

What does Anna do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Anna visit includes: an enthusiastic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child (her new best friend!), live singing performances, a friendship ceremony with a keepsake token, adventure-themed games and activities like "Find Elsa" and snowman building, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, storytime about Arendelle, and additional activities.

Is your Anna performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Anna come for parties?

Anna can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
