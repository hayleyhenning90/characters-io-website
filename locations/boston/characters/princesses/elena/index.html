About Our Elena

The Ultimate Latina Princess Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Elena of Avalor from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the brave crown princess to life. Our Elena entertainers are actresses who have studied the series extensively, perfecting Elena's voice, her adventurous spirit, courageous leadership, and warm celebration of Latina heritage.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Elena costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation featuring her iconic red flowing gown with gold accents, her royal scepter, and beautiful accessories that children recognize and love from the beloved Disney series. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Elena apart is her bilingual abilities and cultural authenticity. Our Elena performers can incorporate Spanish phrases, songs, and cultural elements that make Latino families feel seen and celebrated. She represents courage, family values, and the importance of learning from mistakes—lessons that resonate with children of all backgrounds.

Perfect for Cultural Celebrations and Birthday Parties

An Elena of Avalor birthday party is perfect for children ages 4-10 who love adventure and strong princess characters. Our Elena character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a community center—into the magical kingdom of Avalor. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special royal friend, and spends the entire visit creating adventurous memories.

The royal leadership activities are always a highlight. Elena teaches children about courage and making good decisions, leads exciting adventure games and quests, and shares stories of her kingdom. Then she leads the group in Latin-inspired dance celebrations, treasure hunts through Avalor, and festive activities that honor her cultural heritage.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Elena of Avalor

How much does it cost to hire Elena of Avalor for a birthday party?

Elena of Avalor party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What makes Elena of Avalor special for birthday parties?

Elena is Disney's first Latina princess, making her an incredibly meaningful choice for cultural celebrations and families who want their children to see themselves represented. She embodies leadership, bravery, family values, and learning from mistakes—powerful lessons that resonate with children of all backgrounds. Her adventurous spirit and strong character appeal to kids who want an action-oriented princess!

What ages is Elena of Avalor best for?

Our Elena character is perfect for children ages 4-10, with the sweet spot being 5-9 year olds who love adventure and the Elena of Avalor series. Her themes of leadership, courage, and family resonate especially well with this age group. Our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger children who might be shy to older kids who want to participate in adventure games.

Can your Elena of Avalor speak Spanish at the party?

Yes! Our Elena performers are bilingual and can incorporate Spanish phrases, greetings, songs, and cultural elements into the party experience. This makes Elena perfect for bilingual families, quinceañera celebrations, Dia de los Muertos events, and any cultural celebration where you want to honor Latina heritage.

How far in advance should I book Elena of Avalor?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Elena is a popular princess character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, Dia de los Muertos, Hispanic Heritage Month, or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead.

Can I book Elena with other princess characters?

Absolutely! Elena pairs wonderfully with other adventurous princess characters. Our most popular pairing is Elena with Mirabel from Encanto for a celebration of Latina heritage and family. She also pairs beautifully with Moana for an adventurous duo, or with Jasmine for a magical princess combination. Contact us for duo package pricing.

What does Elena of Avalor do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Elena visit includes: a grand royal entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, leadership and adventure games, Latin-inspired dance activities, storytelling about Avalor, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, royal training activities, and additional adventures.

Is your Elena of Avalor performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.
