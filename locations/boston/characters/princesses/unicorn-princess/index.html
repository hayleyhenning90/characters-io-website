Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Unicorn Princess

How much does it cost to hire Unicorn Princess for a birthday party?

Unicorn Princess party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Unicorn Princess wear to parties?

Our Unicorn Princess wears a stunning pastel rainbow gown in soft pinks, purples, and iridescent shades, adorned with sparkles and glitter accents throughout. The centerpiece is her magnificent unicorn horn headpiece, which children absolutely adore. She also carries a sparkling wand for magical wish-making. The costume is professionally made and photographs beautifully!

What ages is Unicorn Princess best for?

Our Unicorn Princess character is perfect for children ages 3-8, with the sweet spot being 4-6 year olds who love all things magical, sparkly, and rainbow-colored. The fantasy princess theme appeals strongly to young imaginations. Our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to participate in all the magical activities!

How far in advance should I book Unicorn Princess for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Unicorn Princess is very popular for unicorn-themed and rainbow parties, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can Unicorn Princess do glitter tattoos?

Yes! Our Unicorn Princess can apply beautiful glitter tattoos featuring unicorns, rainbows, stars, hearts, and other magical designs. Glitter tattoos are one of the most popular activities at unicorn-themed parties—children love the sparkle and getting to choose their own design. The tattoos are safe, non-toxic, and typically last 2-4 days.

What does Unicorn Princess do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Unicorn Princess visit includes: a magical entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, wish-making ceremony with her sparkling wand, glitter tattoo applications, rainbow craft activities, enchanted dancing and games, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities, games, and storytime about unicorn adventures.

Is your Unicorn Princess performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Can I book Unicorn Princess with other characters?

Absolutely! Unicorn Princess pairs beautifully with other magical characters. She's wonderful with Tinker Bell for a fairy and fantasy theme, with Rapunzel for adventurous dreamers, or with any of our other princess characters for a royal celebration. Duo bookings receive a discount. We also offer Unicorn Princess paired with a mermaid princess for an extra magical experience!
