Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring a Jedi

How much does it cost to hire a Jedi for a birthday party?

Jedi party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like a Darth Vader opponent or face painting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does a Jedi do at the party?

Our Jedi leads a lightsaber training academy where kids learn combat moves, teaches Force push games, guides Jedi meditation exercises, conducts a special padawan ceremony for the birthday child, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song with wisdom, and makes the birthday child feel like they've joined the Jedi Order. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

What ages is a Jedi best for?

Our Jedi character is perfect for children ages 4-14, with the sweet spot being 5-12 year olds who love Star Wars. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who are in awe of meeting a real Jedi to older kids who want to learn advanced lightsaber moves. We've even done Jedi appearances for teens and adults who are lifelong Star Wars fans!

How far in advance should I book a Jedi for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Our Jedi is one of our most popular Star Wars characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book a Jedi with other Star Wars characters?

Absolutely! Booking a Jedi with Darth Vader creates an epic Light vs Dark battle experience that kids love. We also offer Jedi paired with Rey or Princess Leia for a Light Side team. When you hire multiple characters, they interact and create immersive galactic adventures for the kids. The second character is discounted when booked together.

What does a typical Jedi visit include?

A typical 1-hour Jedi visit includes: a heroic arrival in character, special greeting for the birthday child as a new padawan, lightsaber training academy session, Force power games, Jedi meditation, padawan ceremony, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, a galactic adventure story, and additional activities.

Is your Jedi performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can a Jedi come for parties?

Our Jedi can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
