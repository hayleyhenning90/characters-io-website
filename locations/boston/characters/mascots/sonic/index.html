Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Sonic

How much does it cost to hire Sonic for a birthday party?

Sonic party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What games does Sonic play at parties?

Our Sonic character leads exciting games inspired by the video game! Favorites include speed races, ring collecting challenges, obstacle courses, and dance parties. Kids love experiencing their favorite Sonic adventures in real life!

What ages is Sonic best for?

Our Sonic character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being kids who love video games and action-packed entertainment. Our performer is skilled at engaging children of all ages, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Sonic for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Sonic is a popular video game character, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book other video game characters with Sonic?

Absolutely! Sonic pairs great with other popular mascot characters. When you book multiple characters together, they interact and create an even more exciting party atmosphere. Contact us for multi-character package pricing.

What does Sonic do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Sonic visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Sonic-themed games like speed races and ring collecting, a dance party, plenty of high-fives and photos with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Sonic performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Sonic come for parties?

Sonic can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
