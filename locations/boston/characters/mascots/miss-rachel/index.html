Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Miss Rachel

How much does it cost to hire Miss Rachel for a birthday party?

Miss Rachel party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What songs does Miss Rachel sing at parties?

Our Miss Rachel character performs beloved educational songs that toddlers recognize and love! This includes classic nursery rhymes like "Wheels on the Bus" and "Itsy Bitsy Spider," ABCs, counting songs, and interactive sing-alongs that encourage speech development and participation from little ones.

What ages is Miss Rachel best for?

Our Miss Rachel character is perfect for children ages 1-4, making her ideal for first, second, third, and fourth birthday parties. Her gentle, patient approach is specifically designed for toddlers, including those who might be overwhelmed by more energetic characters.

How far in advance should I book Miss Rachel for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Miss Rachel is extremely popular with parents of toddlers, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Does Miss Rachel help with speech development at parties?

Yes! Our Miss Rachel performer incorporates speech-friendly techniques into party activities. She encourages sounds, words, and participation through songs and interactive games that naturally support early language development - all in a fun, pressure-free environment.

Is Miss Rachel good for shy toddlers?

Absolutely! Our Miss Rachel performer is specially trained to work with shy and hesitant toddlers. She uses gentle encouragement, never forces interaction, and creates a warm, welcoming environment. Many parents tell us their shy little ones opened up beautifully during the party!

What activities does Miss Rachel do at parties?

A typical Miss Rachel visit includes: a warm, gentle entrance, sing-along songs and nursery rhymes, ABCs and counting activities, bubble time, instrument play, gentle dancing, plenty of encouragement and positive reinforcement, photo opportunities, and helping with the birthday song and cake. 2-hour packages include extended activities.

Can Miss Rachel come to daycare or preschool events?

Yes! Miss Rachel is perfect for daycare centers, preschools, library events, mommy-and-me classes, and educational gatherings. She can adapt her program for larger groups and loves engaging with classrooms full of little learners. Contact us for special group event pricing.
