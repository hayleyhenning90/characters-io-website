Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Captain Marvel

How much does it cost to hire Captain Marvel for a birthday party?

Captain Marvel party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Captain Marvel do at parties?

Our Captain Marvel leads flight training sessions where kids learn heroic poses and superhero landings, cosmic power games and challenges, strength activities that build confidence, and empowerment exercises. She also does photo opportunities, helps with cake and candles, and stays fully in character throughout, inspiring children with messages of determination, courage, and going higher, further, faster.

What ages is Captain Marvel best for?

Our Captain Marvel character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love Marvel superhero stories. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little shy to older kids who want to learn every superhero move. Captain Marvel's message of determination and never giving up resonates with children of all ages!

How far in advance should I book Captain Marvel for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Captain Marvel is one of our most popular superhero characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends or summer dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Captain Marvel with other superheroes?

Absolutely! Captain Marvel pairs perfectly with other superhero characters for an epic party experience. Popular combinations include Captain Marvel with Wonder Woman for ultimate girl power, with Black Widow for a Marvel heroines duo, or with Spider-Man for an Avengers team-up. We also offer Captain Marvel alongside other Marvel heroes like Iron Man or Thor for mixed superhero parties.

What does Captain Marvel do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Captain Marvel visit includes: a heroic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, flight training session, cosmic power games and activities, strength challenges, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, empowerment activities, and additional cosmic adventures.

Is your Captain Marvel performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Captain Marvel come for parties?

Captain Marvel can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, gymnasium, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
