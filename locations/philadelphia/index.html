Philly FAQ

Philadelphia Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Philadelphia?

Character party packages in Philadelphia start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within Greater Philadelphia. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Philadelphia do you serve?

We serve all of Greater Philadelphia including King of Prussia, Cherry Hill, the Main Line (Ardmore, Wayne, Bryn Mawr), Media, West Chester, Doylestown, Conshohocken, and South Jersey.

How far in advance should I book in Philadelphia?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Philadelphia parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

What do the characters do at Philly parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Face painting is included; balloons are available for an additional fee.

Do you perform at venues or just homes in Philadelphia?

We perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout Greater Philadelphia. We can come to any venue that's accessible!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
