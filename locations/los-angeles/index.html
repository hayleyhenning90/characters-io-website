Los Angeles Party Character FAQ

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Los Angeles?

Party character packages in Los Angeles start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available at $50 per half hour. Pricing may vary based on character and travel distance within LA County.

What areas in Los Angeles do you serve?

We serve all of Los Angeles County including Beverly Hills, Hollywood, Santa Monica, Pasadena, Burbank, Glendale, Long Beach, Torrance, Culver City, West Hollywood, Malibu, Westwood, Downtown LA, and all surrounding areas.

How far in advance should I book a character in Los Angeles?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Los Angeles parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check availability.

What do party characters do at LA birthday parties?

Our Los Angeles party characters provide interactive entertainment including singing (for princess characters), dancing, games, face painting, balloon twisting, photo opportunities, and leading the happy birthday celebration. Activities vary by character type and package selected.

Do you serve venues in Los Angeles or just home parties?

We serve both! Our characters perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout Los Angeles. We can come to any venue that's accessible.
