FAQ

Los Angeles Party Questions

Do you really account for LA traffic?

Absolutely! Our performers build 30-60 minute traffic buffers depending on route and time of day. We monitor real-time traffic from the 405 to the 10, PCH to the 110. We've been serving LA since 2008 and understand the city's traffic patterns intimately. We're on time, every time.

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Los Angeles?

Character party packages in Los Angeles start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within LA County. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

Can characters perform outdoors in LA heat?

Yes, with proper planning! For outdoor events, we recommend morning or late afternoon slots (before 11am or after 4pm). Our performers bring cooling gear, take scheduled breaks, and we have protocols for extreme heat days. Many LA families book us for beachside, park, and backyard pool parties successfully.

Which LA neighborhoods do you serve?

We serve all of Los Angeles County! From downtown LA to Malibu beaches, Pasadena to Long Beach, Beverly Hills to Burbank. We regularly perform in Santa Monica, Hollywood, Glendale, Torrance, Culver City, Manhattan Beach, and everywhere in between.

How far in advance should I book for an LA party?

For LA parties, we recommend booking 4-6 weeks ahead, especially for weekends and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. LA is one of our busiest markets, and prime Saturday/Sunday slots fill quickly. We do accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

What's included in a Los Angeles character visit?

Every booking includes a professional performer in full costume, interactive entertainment (singing, dancing, games), face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Standard visits are 1 hour. We provide all materials needed - you just enjoy the party!
