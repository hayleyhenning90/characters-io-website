LA FAQ

Los Angeles Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Los Angeles?

Character party packages in Los Angeles start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within LA County. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Los Angeles do you serve?

We serve all of Los Angeles County including Beverly Hills, Hollywood, Santa Monica, Pasadena, Burbank, Glendale, Long Beach, Torrance, Culver City, West Hollywood, Malibu, Downtown LA, and all surrounding areas.

How far in advance should I book in Los Angeles?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Los Angeles parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

What do the characters do at LA parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Face painting is included; balloons are available for an additional fee.

Do you perform at venues or just homes in LA?

We perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout Los Angeles. We can come to any venue that's accessible - Griffith Park, Santa Monica Pier area, or your backyard!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
