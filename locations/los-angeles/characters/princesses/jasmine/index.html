About Our Jasmine

The Ultimate Aladdin Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Jasmine from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the Arabian Princess to life. Our Jasmine entertainers are actresses who have studied the Aladdin films extensively, perfecting Jasmine's voice, independent spirit, and adventurous personality.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Jasmine costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic turquoise outfit, complete with hand-sewn gold details, flowing chiffon accents, signature headband, and the beautiful jewelry that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Jasmine apart is her adventurous spirit and engaging activities. She leads children on magic carpet adventures, teaches Arabian dance moves, and performs "A Whole New World" and other Aladdin favorites. Children's faces light up when Jasmine shares her message of following your dreams and being true to yourself.

Perfect for Aladdin-Themed Birthday Parties

An Aladdin birthday party with Jasmine transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a restaurant—into the magical kingdom of Agrabah. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special friend, and spends the entire visit creating magical moments filled with adventure and wonder.

The wishing lamp ceremony is always a highlight. Jasmine helps the birthday child make special wishes with an enchanted lamp, creating a memorable moment they'll treasure. Then she leads the group in Aladdin-themed games like magic carpet races, the Cave of Wonders treasure hunt, and Arabian dance activities.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Jasmine

How much does it cost to hire Jasmine for a birthday party?

Jasmine party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What songs does Jasmine sing at parties?

Our Jasmine performers sing "A Whole New World" and other beloved Aladdin favorites. She also leads children in fun Arabian-themed musical activities and dance sessions that bring the magic of Agrabah to life!

What ages is Jasmine best for?

Our Jasmine character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love the Aladdin story. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to hear about adventures in Agrabah. Jasmine's message of independence and following your dreams resonates with children of all ages!

How far in advance should I book Jasmine for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Jasmine is a beloved princess character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Jasmine and Aladdin together?

Absolutely! Booking Jasmine and Aladdin together creates the ultimate Aladdin party experience. They interact as the beloved couple from the films, perform together, and create twice the magic. The Aladdin add-on is discounted when booked with Jasmine. We also offer Jasmine paired with other princesses like Moana, Rapunzel, or Ariel for a "princess party" theme.

What does Jasmine do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Jasmine visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, magic carpet adventure games, Arabian dance session, a treasure hunt inspired by the Cave of Wonders, the wishing lamp ceremony, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, a storytime, and additional activities.

Is your Jasmine performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Jasmine come for parties?

Jasmine can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
