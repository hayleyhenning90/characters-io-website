When you hire Aurora from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the Sleeping Beauty princess to life. Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Aurora

How much does it cost to hire Aurora for a birthday party?

Aurora party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Aurora actually sing "Once Upon a Dream" live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Aurora entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "Once Upon a Dream" and other classic fairy tale songs performed in person by the Sleeping Beauty princess herself. It's always a highlight of the party!

What ages is Aurora best for?

Our Aurora character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love classic fairy tales and elegant princesses. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who appreciate the timeless beauty of Sleeping Beauty.

How far in advance should I book Aurora for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Aurora is a beloved classic princess character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Aurora with other classic princesses?

Absolutely! Aurora pairs beautifully with other classic fairy tale princesses. Our most popular pairings include Aurora with Cinderella for a classic princess duo, Aurora with Belle for a fairy tale celebration, or Aurora with Snow White for a timeless royal experience. Multiple princess packages are discounted when booked together.

What does Aurora do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Aurora visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, live singing of "Once Upon a Dream," enchanting fairy tale storytime, princess etiquette lessons with curtsying practice, dancing and wand magic activities, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, additional stories, and extended activities.

Is your Aurora performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Aurora come for parties?

Aurora can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
