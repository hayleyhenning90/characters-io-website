About Our Mary Poppins

The Ultimate Magical Nanny Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Mary Poppins from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings P.L. Travers' beloved magical nanny to life. Our Mary Poppins entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting her prim and proper demeanor, quick wit, and the magical way she makes even the most mundane tasks an adventure.

Every detail matters when creating authentic Mary Poppins party entertainment. Our costume is a premium recreation of the iconic Edwardian look, complete with the signature blue coat, white high-collared blouse, elegant hat with flowers, the famous parrot-handled umbrella, and the magical carpet bag that children recognize and adore. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures capture that classic elegance.

What sets our Mary Poppins apart is her interactive storytelling and musical talent. Children are captivated as she leads them in beloved songs, teaches proper manners with delightful humor, and creates magic with her carpet bag and umbrella. Her warm yet no-nonsense personality perfectly balances fun and elegance.

Perfect for Tea Parties and Classic Themed Events

A Mary Poppins birthday party is a timeless theme perfect for children ages 3-10. Our Mary Poppins character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a garden, a party venue, or a tea room—into magical Cherry Tree Lane. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as her special charge, and spends the entire visit creating enchanting, practically perfect moments.

The tea party experience is always a highlight. Mary Poppins gathers the children for a proper English tea celebration with lessons in etiquette, silly songs, and whimsical games. Then she leads sing-alongs of beloved classics, performs carpet bag magic, and teaches the children that "in every job that must be done, there is an element of fun!"

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Mary Poppins

How much does it cost to hire Mary Poppins for a birthday party?

Mary Poppins party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What songs does Mary Poppins sing at the party?

Our Mary Poppins leads delightful sing-alongs featuring beloved classics like "A Spoonful of Sugar," "Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious," "Let's Go Fly a Kite," and "Jolly Holiday." Children absolutely love learning the tongue-twisting lyrics and singing along with the practically perfect nanny!

What ages is Mary Poppins best for?

Our Mary Poppins character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love classic stories and musical fun. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger children who love the singing and magic to older kids who appreciate the wit and elegance.

What props does Mary Poppins bring to the party?

Our Mary Poppins arrives with her iconic parrot-handled umbrella and magical carpet bag filled with surprises. These signature props are used throughout the party for games, magic moments, and wonderful photo opportunities that capture the classic Mary Poppins experience.

Is Mary Poppins good for tea party themes?

Absolutely! Mary Poppins is the perfect character for tea party themed celebrations. She brings refined British elegance, teaches proper etiquette with a fun and whimsical twist, and creates that classic afternoon tea atmosphere that makes your celebration extra special and memorable.

How far in advance should I book Mary Poppins?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Mary Poppins is a beloved classic character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Mary Poppins with Bert the chimney sweep?

Yes! Booking Mary Poppins with Bert creates the ultimate Cherry Tree Lane experience. Together they lead rooftop adventures, sing duets, and bring twice the magic to your celebration. It's a jolly holiday indeed when this beloved pair appears together!

Is your Mary Poppins performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.
