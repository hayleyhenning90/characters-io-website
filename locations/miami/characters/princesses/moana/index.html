About Our Moana

The Ultimate Ocean Adventure Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Moana from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the brave wayfinder to life. Our Moana entertainers are actresses and singers who have studied the film extensively, perfecting Moana's voice, adventurous spirit, and warm Polynesian heritage.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Moana costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation featuring the iconic red top and tapa cloth skirt with authentic Polynesian-inspired patterns, the heart of Te Fiti necklace, and beautiful island accessories that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Moana apart is the live singing. Many princess performers simply play recorded music, but our Moana actually sings "How Far I'll Go," "You're Welcome," "We Know the Way," and other favorites in person. Children's faces light up when they hear their favorite songs performed live by the wayfinder princess herself.

Perfect for Ocean-Themed Birthday Parties

A Moana birthday party is one of the most popular princess party themes for children ages 3-10. Our Moana character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a beach—into Motunui and the vast ocean beyond. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special fellow voyager, and spends the entire visit creating adventurous memories.

The wayfinding ceremony is always a highlight. Moana teaches children how to navigate by the stars, leads ocean adventure games, and shares stories of her journey across the sea. Then she leads the group in island-inspired activities like ocean exploration games, "find the heart of Te Fiti" treasure hunts, and island dance celebrations.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Moana

How much does it cost to hire Moana for a birthday party?

Moana party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Moana actually sing "How Far I'll Go" live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Moana entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "How Far I'll Go," "You're Welcome," "We Know the Way," and other Moana favorites performed in person by the wayfinder princess herself. It's always a highlight of the party!

What ages is Moana best for?

Our Moana character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love ocean adventures and the film. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to learn about wayfinding. Moana's message of bravery and following your heart resonates with children of all ages!

How far in advance should I book Moana for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Moana is a very popular princess character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Moana with other princess characters?

Absolutely! Moana pairs wonderfully with other characters for an ocean-themed celebration. Our most popular pairing is Moana with Ariel (The Little Mermaid) for the ultimate ocean adventure party. She also pairs beautifully with Rapunzel for an adventurous princess duo, or with Elsa for a magical contrast of ocean and ice. Contact us for duo package pricing.

What does Moana do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Moana visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, live singing performances, wayfinding games and ocean adventures, island dance activities, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, storytelling about her voyage, and additional activities.

Is your Moana performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Moana come for parties?

Moana can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, beach, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. Beach and pool parties are especially popular for Moana events! We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
