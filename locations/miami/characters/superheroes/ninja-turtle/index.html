Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring a Ninja Turtle

How much does it cost to hire a Ninja Turtle for a birthday party?

Ninja Turtle party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does the Ninja Turtle do at the party?

Our Ninja Turtle leads martial arts training where kids learn awesome ninja moves and poses. He runs pizza-themed games, exciting villain takedown activities, teamwork challenges, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song, and makes the birthday child feel like a true member of the turtle team. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

Which Ninja Turtle can I book for my party?

We offer all four beloved Ninja Turtles! You can request Leonardo (the leader in blue), Raphael (the tough one in red), Donatello (the tech genius in purple), or Michelangelo (the party dude in orange). Let us know your child's favorite when booking, and we'll make sure the right turtle shows up to save the day.

What ages is the Ninja Turtle best for?

Our Ninja Turtle character is perfect for children ages 4-10, with the sweet spot being 5-8 year olds who love action heroes and adventure. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who love the silly pizza jokes to older kids who want to learn real ninja moves. The TMNT franchise appeals to multiple generations!

Can I book multiple Ninja Turtles for a party?

Absolutely! Booking two or more Ninja Turtles creates the ultimate turtle team experience. Popular combinations include Leonardo with Michelangelo, or all four turtles for an epic TMNT party. When you hire multiple turtles, they interact and create an authentic team-up experience for the kids. Additional characters are discounted when booked together.

How far in advance should I book a Ninja Turtle for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Ninja Turtle is a fan-favorite character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Is your Ninja Turtle performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can the Ninja Turtle come for parties?

Our Ninja Turtle can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, pizza restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
