Miami FAQ

Miami Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Miami?

Character party packages in Miami start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within Miami-Dade and Broward counties. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Miami do you serve?

We serve all of Miami-Dade and Broward counties including Miami, Miami Beach, Coral Gables, Kendall, Doral, Hialeah, Aventura, Brickell, Coconut Grove, Pinecrest, Weston, Pembroke Pines, and Fort Lauderdale.

Do you offer bilingual characters in Miami?

Yes! Many of our Miami performers are bilingual in English and Spanish. Please let us know when booking if you'd prefer a Spanish-speaking character and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

How far in advance should I book in Miami?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Miami parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

Do you perform at outdoor venues in Miami's heat?

We recommend indoor or covered venues during Miami summers for the comfort of our performers and guests. If your party is outdoors, please have a shaded area and water available. Moana is especially popular for pool parties!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
