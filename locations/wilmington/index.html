Wilmington Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Wilmington?

Character party packages in Wilmington start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within New Castle County. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Delaware do you serve?

We serve all of New Castle County including Wilmington, Hockessin, Newark, Greenville, Pike Creek, Bear, Middletown, New Castle, Claymont, and surrounding areas including Elkton, MD.

How far in advance should I book in Delaware?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Delaware parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

What do the characters do at Delaware parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Face painting is included; balloons are available for an additional fee.

Do you perform at venues or just homes in Delaware?

We perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout Delaware. We can come to any venue that's accessible!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
