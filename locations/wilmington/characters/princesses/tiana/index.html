About Our Tiana

The Ultimate Princess and the Frog Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Tiana from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the hardworking New Orleans princess to life. Our Tiana entertainers are actresses who have studied The Princess and the Frog extensively, perfecting Tiana's warmth, determination, and inspiring spirit.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Tiana costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic green lily pad gown, complete with hand-sewn details, elegant gloves, and the beautiful tiara that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Tiana apart is her inspiring message. She teaches children that dreams come true through hard work and determination, while still bringing all the fun and magic of a princess party. Her "Almost There" spirit encourages kids to believe in themselves and work toward their goals.

Perfect for Princess and the Frog-Themed Birthday Parties

A Princess and the Frog birthday party with Tiana is perfect for children who love this heartwarming story. Our Tiana character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a restaurant—into the magical bayou of New Orleans. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special friend, and spends the entire visit creating magical moments.

The wishing star ceremony is always a highlight. Tiana leads children in making wishes on the Evening Star, sharing the magic of dreams while teaching that hard work makes wishes come true. Then she leads the group in New Orleans-themed activities, cooking pretend play, and tells the enchanting story of how she met Prince Naveen.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Tiana

How much does it cost to hire Tiana for a birthday party?

Tiana party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What makes Tiana special for birthday parties?

Tiana brings a unique message of hard work, determination, and following your dreams. She's perfect for parents who want their children inspired by a princess who achieved her goals through dedication. Our Tiana leads cooking pretend play, New Orleans dance activities, and shares her inspiring story while still bringing all the magic and fun of a princess party.

What ages is Tiana best for?

Our Tiana character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love The Princess and the Frog. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to hear about her adventures in New Orleans and the bayou.

How far in advance should I book Tiana for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Tiana with other princesses?

Absolutely! Tiana pairs wonderfully with other princesses for a "princess party" theme. Popular pairings include Tiana with Moana (both strong, independent princesses), Tiana with Belle (both book-smart dreamers), or Tiana with Rapunzel. We offer discounted rates when booking multiple characters together.

What does Tiana do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Tiana visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, cooking and baking pretend play activities, New Orleans-style dance time, a wishing star ceremony, the frog prince story, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, activities, and additional stories.

Is your Tiana performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Tiana come for parties?

Tiana can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
