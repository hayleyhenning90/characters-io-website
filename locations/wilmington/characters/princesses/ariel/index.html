About Our Ariel

The Ultimate Mermaid Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Ariel from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the Little Mermaid princess to life. Our Ariel entertainers are actresses and singers who have studied the films extensively, perfecting Ariel's voice, curious personality, and adventurous spirit.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Ariel costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic mermaid look, complete with flowing red hair, the purple seashell top, and beautiful green tail-inspired skirt that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look magical.

What sets our Ariel apart is the live singing. Many princess performers simply play recorded music, but our Ariel actually sings "Part of Your World," "Under the Sea," "Kiss the Girl," and other Little Mermaid favorites in person. Children's faces light up when they hear their favorite songs performed live by the mermaid princess herself.

Perfect for Mermaid-Themed Birthday Parties

A mermaid birthday party with Ariel is one of the most beloved princess party themes for children ages 2-10. Our Ariel character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a pool party, or a party venue—into an underwater kingdom. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a special mermaid friend, and spends the entire visit creating magical moments.

The under the sea activities are always a highlight. Ariel leads the group in ocean-themed games like the under the sea dance party, mermaid relay races, seashell hunts, and an exciting treasure hunt adventure. She shares stories about life under the sea and teaches children about her collection of human treasures—including her beloved "dinglehopper"!

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Ariel

How much does it cost to hire Ariel for a birthday party?

Ariel party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Ariel actually sing "Part of Your World" live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Ariel entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "Part of Your World," "Under the Sea," "Kiss the Girl," and other Little Mermaid favorites performed in person by the mermaid princess herself. It's always a highlight of the party!

What ages is Ariel best for?

Our Ariel character is perfect for children ages 2-10, with the sweet spot being 3-7 year olds who love The Little Mermaid. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to chat about life under the sea. We've even done Ariel appearances for teens and adults who grew up with the film!

How far in advance should I book Ariel for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Ariel is one of our most popular princess characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Ariel with other water-themed characters?

Absolutely! Ariel pairs beautifully with Moana for an ocean-themed party, as both characters share a love for the sea. We also offer Ariel paired with Rapunzel, Elsa, or other princesses for a classic princess party theme. Having two characters doubles the magic and allows for more interactive activities and games.

What does Ariel do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Ariel visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, live singing performances, an under the sea dance party, mermaid-themed games and activities including a treasure hunt, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, a storytime about life under the sea, and additional activities.

Is your Ariel performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Ariel come for parties?

Ariel can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, pool party, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. She's especially popular for pool parties and beach events! We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
