Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Bluey

How much does it cost to hire Bluey for a birthday party?

Bluey party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What games does Bluey play at parties?

Our Bluey character leads classic games from the beloved TV show! Favorites include Keepy Uppy (the balloon game from the first episode), Magic Xylophone (freeze game), dance parties, and other imaginative play activities. Kids love experiencing their favorite Bluey episodes in real life!

What ages is Bluey best for?

Our Bluey character is perfect for children ages 2-7, with the sweet spot being preschoolers and early elementary kids who are big fans of the show. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging toddlers and young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Bluey for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Bluey is our most requested mascot character, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Bluey and Bingo together?

Absolutely! Booking both Heeler sisters is our most popular mascot duo package. When you hire Bluey and Bingo together, they interact as the playful siblings from the show, lead games together, and create twice the Heeler family fun. The Bingo add-on is discounted when booked with Bluey.

What does Bluey do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Bluey visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Bluey-themed games like Keepy Uppy and Magic Xylophone, a dance party, plenty of hugs and cuddles, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Bluey performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Bluey come for parties?

Bluey can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
