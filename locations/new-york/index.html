NYC FAQ

New York Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in New York?

Character party packages in New York start at $249 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within NYC and nearby areas. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in New York do you serve?

We serve all five boroughs (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, Staten Island), Long Island, Westchester, and the tri-state area including Jersey City, Hoboken, and parts of Connecticut.

How far in advance should I book in New York?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for NYC parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

What do the characters do at NYC parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, story time, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration. Face painting is included; balloons are available for an additional fee.

Do you perform at apartments or just venues in NYC?

We perform at apartments, homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout NYC. We're experienced with NYC apartment parties and building logistics!

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
