Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Thor

How much does it cost to hire Thor for a birthday party?

Thor party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Thor do at the party?

Our Thor leads hammer training sessions where kids learn to wield Mjolnir. He runs lightning games, teaches Asgardian battle poses, conducts a worthiness test, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song, and makes the birthday child feel like a true warrior of Asgard. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

What ages is Thor best for?

Our Thor character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love Marvel superheroes and the Avengers. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little in awe to older kids who want to learn warrior moves. We've even done Thor appearances for teens and adults who are lifelong Marvel fans!

How far in advance should I book Thor for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Thor is one of our most popular Marvel characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Thor with other Avengers?

Absolutely! Booking Thor with Captain America, Hulk, or Spider-Man is a popular combo for Avengers parties. When you hire multiple heroes, they interact and create an epic team-up experience for the kids. The second character is discounted when booked together.

What does a typical Thor visit include?

A typical 1-hour Thor visit includes: a dramatic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, hammer training session, lightning games, Asgardian battle poses, worthiness test, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, tales from Asgard storytelling, and additional activities.

Is your Thor performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Thor come for parties?

Thor can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
