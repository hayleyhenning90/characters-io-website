About Our Rapunzel

The Ultimate Tangled Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Rapunzel from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the adventurous princess to life. Our Rapunzel entertainers are actresses who have studied Tangled extensively, perfecting Rapunzel's curious nature, creative spirit, and warm-hearted personality.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Rapunzel costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic purple dress from Tangled, complete with the lace-up bodice, puff sleeves, and beautiful embroidery that children recognize and love. The flowing golden braid decorated with flowers is always a show-stopper, and the costume photographs beautifully.

What sets our Rapunzel apart is the interactive activities. Children love getting their hair braided with flowers just like Rapunzel's, participating in painting activities that channel her artistic spirit, and making lantern wishes inspired by the most magical scene in Tangled. Our Rapunzel brings the adventurous, curious, and creative energy that makes the character so beloved.

Perfect for Tangled-Themed Birthday Parties

A Tangled birthday party with Rapunzel is perfect for girls ages 3-10 who dream of adventure and creativity. Then she leads the group in dancing like they're in the village square, painting activities, and of course, hair braiding for anyone who wants the Rapunzel look!

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Rapunzel

How much does it cost to hire Rapunzel for a birthday party?

Rapunzel party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Rapunzel do hair braiding?

Yes! Our Rapunzel performers are skilled at creating beautiful braids and can style each child's hair with flowers and ribbons, recreating Rapunzel's iconic look. Hair braiding is one of the most popular activities at Rapunzel parties—children love getting the "Rapunzel treatment" with their very own flower-adorned braid!

What ages is Rapunzel best for?

Our Rapunzel character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love Tangled. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to talk about the kingdom of Corona and Pascal the chameleon. The activities like hair braiding and painting appeal to a wide age range!

How far in advance should I book Rapunzel for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Rapunzel is one of our most popular princess characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Rapunzel with Flynn Rider?

Yes! Booking Rapunzel and Flynn Rider together creates an amazing Tangled experience. They interact as the beloved couple from the film, creating twice the adventure and fun. The Flynn Rider add-on is discounted when booked with Rapunzel. We also offer Rapunzel paired with other princesses like Elsa, Cinderella, or Belle for a "princess party" theme.

What does Rapunzel do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Rapunzel visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, hair braiding with flowers for children who want it, a painting or creative activity, lantern wish-making inspired by Tangled, dancing, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities, games, and storytime.

Is your Rapunzel performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Rapunzel come for parties?

Rapunzel can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
