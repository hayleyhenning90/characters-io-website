About Our Alice

The Ultimate Wonderland Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Alice from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings Lewis Carroll's beloved heroine to life. Our Alice entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting her curious nature, polite demeanor, and sense of wonder at the nonsensical world around her.

Every detail matters when creating authentic Wonderland party entertainment. Our Alice costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic blue dress with white pinafore apron, complete with the signature black headband and blonde hair that children recognize and adore. The costume photographs wonderfully, ensuring your party pictures look absolutely magical.

What sets our Alice apart is her interactive storytelling and whimsical games. Children are captivated as she shares tales of her curious adventures, teaches them Wonderland riddles, and leads them through tea party games. Her warm personality and genuine wonder make every child feel like they've tumbled down the rabbit hole into a magical adventure.

Perfect for Wonderland-Themed Birthday Parties

An Alice in Wonderland birthday party is a beloved theme for children ages 3-10. Our Alice character appearance transforms any venue—your home, a park, a party venue, or a garden—into magical Wonderland. She arrives in character, greets the birthday child as a fellow curious adventurer, and spends the entire visit creating enchanting moments.

The mad tea party is always a highlight. Alice gathers the children around the table for a very merry unbirthday celebration filled with silly songs, musical chairs, and Wonderland nonsense. Then she leads the group in croquet games, riddle contests, and storytelling that transport children into the topsy-turvy world of Wonderland.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Alice

How much does it cost to hire Alice for a birthday party?

Alice party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Alice do during the tea party?

The mad tea party is the magical highlight of every Alice appearance! She gathers all the children around for a very merry unbirthday celebration with silly songs, musical chairs, and Wonderland nonsense. Alice shares riddles, leads tea party etiquette lessons with a whimsical twist, and makes everyone feel like honored guests at the most curious party in all of Wonderland!

What ages is Alice best for?

Our Alice character is perfect for children ages 3-10, with the sweet spot being 4-8 year olds who love whimsy and adventure. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger children who love the colorful costume to older kids who enjoy the riddles and wordplay. The curious magic of Wonderland appeals to anyone with imagination!

How far in advance should I book Alice for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Alice is a popular character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Alice with the Mad Hatter or Queen of Hearts?

Absolutely! Booking Alice with the Mad Hatter is a magical combination that brings Wonderland to life. Together they host the ultimate tea party, lead croquet games, and create twice the whimsy. We also offer Alice paired with the Queen of Hearts for a royally fun "Off with their heads!" experience that children love.

What does Alice do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Alice visit includes: a magical entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, the enchanting mad tea party celebration, croquet games, Wonderland riddles and brain teasers, unbirthday celebration, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, storytime about her Wonderland adventures, and additional activities.

Is your Alice performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Alice come for parties?

Alice can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. Garden settings are especially magical for Wonderland parties! We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
