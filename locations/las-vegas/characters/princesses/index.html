Princess Party FAQ

Do your princesses actually sing?

Yes! All our princess performers are trained vocalists who sing live versions of iconic songs from their movies. Elsa sings "Let It Go," Moana sings "How Far I'll Go," and so on.

Can I book two princesses together?

Absolutely! Elsa and Anna are a popular duo, as are Ariel and a prince, or any combination you'd like. Contact us for duo pricing.

What ages are princess parties good for?

Princess parties are perfect for children ages 2-10, with peak popularity around ages 3-7. Our performers adapt their energy and activities to match the age group.

How long do princess visits last?

Standard visits are 1 hour, which includes all activities. You can book additional time in 30-minute increments for larger parties or more activities.
