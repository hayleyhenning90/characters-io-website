Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Blippi

How much does it cost to hire Blippi for a birthday party?

Blippi party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like science kits or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Blippi do at parties?

Our Blippi character leads high-energy educational games and activities just like on the show! Favorites include learning about colors and shapes, vehicle and excavator themed games, simple science experiments, dance parties, and interactive exploration activities. Kids love learning while having a blast with Blippi!

What ages is Blippi best for?

Our Blippi character is perfect for children ages 2-6, with preschoolers being the biggest fans. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging toddlers and young children with high-energy, educational entertainment that keeps them excited and learning throughout the party.

How far in advance should I book Blippi for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Blippi is one of our most requested educational characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Does Blippi wear the iconic orange and blue outfit?

Yes! Our Blippi performer arrives in the complete signature look with the iconic orange and blue outfit, suspenders, bow tie, and cap that kids instantly recognize from the show. The authentic appearance creates magical moments and excited reactions from young fans.

What does Blippi do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Blippi visit includes: an exciting high-energy entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, educational games about colors/shapes/vehicles, a dance party, exploration activities, plenty of high-fives and hugs, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add science experiments and more activities.

Is your Blippi performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Blippi come for parties?

Blippi can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, preschool, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
