When you hire Chase from Characters.io, you're bringing Adventure Bay's bravest police pup to life for your child's special day. Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Chase

How much does it cost to hire Chase for a birthday party?

Chase party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Chase do at parties?

Our Chase character leads exciting activities from the beloved TV show! Favorites include rescue missions, the Pup Pup Boogie dance party, lookout tower games, and a special badge ceremony. Kids love experiencing their favorite Paw Patrol adventures in real life!

What ages is Chase best for?

Our Chase character is perfect for children ages 2-6, with the sweet spot being toddlers and preschoolers who are big fans of the show. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Chase for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Chase is one of our most requested mascot characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book multiple Paw Patrol characters together?

Absolutely! Booking Chase with Marshall or Skye is one of our most popular mascot duo packages. When you hire multiple Paw Patrol pups, they interact as teammates from the show, lead rescue missions together, and create the ultimate Adventure Bay experience. Additional pups are discounted when booked with Chase.

What does Chase do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Chase visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Paw Patrol-themed rescue missions and games, the Pup Pup Boogie dance party, badge ceremony, plenty of hugs and high-fives, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Chase performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Chase come for parties?

Chase can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
