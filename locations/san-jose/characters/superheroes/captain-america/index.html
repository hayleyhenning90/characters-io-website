Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Captain America

How much does it cost to hire Captain America for a birthday party?

Captain America party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Captain America do at the party?

Our Captain America leads shield training sessions where kids learn superhero techniques. He runs an exciting obstacle course, conducts team leader games, performs a hero pledge ceremony, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song, and makes the birthday child feel like a true Avenger. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

What ages is Captain America best for?

Our Captain America character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love superheroes and the Avengers. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little in awe to older kids who want to learn hero moves. We've even done Captain America appearances for teens and adults who are lifelong Marvel fans!

How far in advance should I book Captain America for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Captain America is one of our most popular Marvel superhero characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Captain America with other Avengers?

Absolutely! Booking Captain America with Spider-Man, Thor, or Iron Man is a popular combo for Avengers-themed parties. When you hire multiple heroes, they interact and create an epic team-up experience for the kids. The second character is discounted when booked together, making it an amazing value for the ultimate superhero party.

What does a typical Captain America visit include?

A typical 1-hour Captain America visit includes: a heroic entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, shield training session, obstacle course challenge, team leader games, hero pledge ceremony, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, an Avengers mission adventure story, and additional activities.

Is your Captain America performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Captain America come for parties?

Captain America can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
