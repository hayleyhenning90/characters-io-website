Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Mal

How much does it cost to hire Mal for a birthday party?

Mal party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Mal do at parties?

Our Mal leads exciting activities including spell casting sessions, dance battles with Descendants choreography, villain makeovers, good vs evil games, and photo opportunities. She brings the Isle of the Lost adventure to life and creates an atmosphere of edgy fun and self-discovery.

What ages is Mal best for?

Our Mal character is perfect for children ages 6-14, with the sweet spot being 8-12 year olds who love the Descendants movies. Our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who adore Mal's purple hair to tweens who appreciate the villain kid attitude and fashion.

How far in advance should I book Mal for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Mal is very popular, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Mal with Evie or other Descendants characters?

Absolutely! Booking Mal and Evie together is a popular duo package. When you hire both, they interact as best friends from the Isle of the Lost, creating twice the magic and fun. We also offer Mal paired with Uma or other Descendants characters for the ultimate villain kid celebration.

What does Mal do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Mal visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, spell casting lessons, dance battles with Descendants moves, good vs evil games, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add villain makeovers, more games, and extended dance time.

Is your Mal performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Mal come for parties?

Mal can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
