Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Minnie Mouse

How much does it cost to hire Minnie Mouse for a birthday party?

Minnie Mouse party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Minnie Mouse do at parties?

Our Minnie Mouse character leads bow decorating activities inspired by Minnie's Bow-Toons, hosts polka dot dance parties, runs fashion shows, and creates magical photo opportunities. Kids love experiencing the Disney magic with such a stylish sweetheart!

What ages is Minnie Mouse best for?

Our Minnie Mouse character is perfect for children ages 1-7, with the sweet spot being toddlers and preschoolers who are big fans of Minnie's Bow-Toons and classic Disney. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging very young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Minnie Mouse for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Minnie Mouse is Disney's sweetheart and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Minnie Mouse and Mickey Mouse together?

Absolutely! Booking Minnie and Mickey together is our most popular Disney duo package. When you hire both characters, they interact as the beloved couple from the show, lead games together, and create twice the Disney magic. The Mickey add-on is discounted when booked with Minnie.

What does Minnie Mouse do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Minnie Mouse visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, bow decorating activity, polka dot dance party, character meet and greet with all guests, plenty of hugs and photos, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add fashion shows and more activities.

Is your Minnie Mouse performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Minnie Mouse come for parties?

Minnie Mouse can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
