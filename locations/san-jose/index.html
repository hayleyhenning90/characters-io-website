Bay Area FAQ

San Jose Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in San Jose?

Character party packages in San Jose start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within the South Bay. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in San Jose do you serve?

We serve all of the South Bay including San Jose, Santa Clara, Sunnyvale, Mountain View, Palo Alto, Cupertino, Milpitas, Fremont, Campbell, Los Gatos, Saratoga, Morgan Hill, and all surrounding areas.

Do you serve corporate events at tech companies?

Yes! We regularly perform at corporate family days and company events throughout Silicon Valley. Our characters have appeared at Google, Apple, Facebook, and many other Bay Area tech companies for employee family events.

How far in advance should I book in San Jose?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Bay Area parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

Do you perform at parks in the Bay Area?

Yes! We perform at homes, parks, restaurants, party venues, community centers, schools, and corporate events throughout the South Bay. Happy Hollow, Kelley Park, and other San Jose parks are popular choices.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
