Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Cocomelon JJ

How much does it cost to hire Cocomelon JJ for a birthday party?

Cocomelon JJ party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like bubble machines or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What songs does Cocomelon JJ sing at parties?

Our Cocomelon JJ character leads singalongs of all the beloved nursery rhymes from the show! Favorites include Yes Yes Vegetables, Bath Song, Wheels on the Bus, Baby Shark, ABCs, and more. Toddlers love singing along to songs they recognize from YouTube!

What ages is Cocomelon JJ best for?

Our Cocomelon JJ character is perfect for children ages 1-5, with the sweet spot being toddlers who are devoted fans of the show. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging babies and toddlers, including those who might be a bit shy at first with gentle, patient interaction.

How far in advance should I book Cocomelon JJ for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Cocomelon JJ is extremely popular for toddler birthday parties, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book multiple characters with Cocomelon JJ?

Absolutely! Cocomelon JJ pairs wonderfully with other beloved kids characters. Popular combinations include JJ with Bluey, Paw Patrol characters, or Baby Shark. Adding a second character creates even more excitement and entertainment for the little ones.

What does Cocomelon JJ do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Cocomelon JJ visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, nursery rhyme singalongs, dance party, bubble fun, learning games, plenty of hugs and cuddles, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more songs and activities.

Is your Cocomelon JJ performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Cocomelon JJ come for parties?

Cocomelon JJ can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, daycare, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
