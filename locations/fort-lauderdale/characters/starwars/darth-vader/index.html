The Ultimate Star Wars Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Darth Vader from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the Dark Lord of the Sith to life. Our Darth Vader entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting Vader's deep breathing, commanding presence, and menacing voice.

Every detail matters when creating authentic Star Wars party entertainment. Our Darth Vader costume is a premium, movie-accurate recreation of the iconic Sith Lord suit, complete with sculpted armor, flowing cape, chest control panel, and the signature helmet that children recognize and love. Kids' faces light up when they meet this legendary villain in person and get to train alongside him.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Darth Vader

How much does it cost to hire Darth Vader for a birthday party?

Darth Vader party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like a Stormtrooper companion or face painting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Darth Vader do at the party?

Our Darth Vader makes a dramatic Imperial March entrance, leads lightsaber training sessions where kids learn combat moves, demonstrates Force powers, runs exciting Jedi vs Sith battle games, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song in his iconic voice, and makes the birthday child feel like they've joined the Dark Side. The experience is fully interactive and engaging for all ages.

What ages is Darth Vader best for?

Our Darth Vader character is perfect for children ages 5-14, with the sweet spot being 6-12 year olds who love Star Wars. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little in awe to older kids who want to learn lightsaber moves. We've even done Darth Vader appearances for teens and adults who are lifelong Star Wars fans!

How far in advance should I book Darth Vader for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Darth Vader is one of our most popular Star Wars characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Darth Vader with other Star Wars characters?

Absolutely! Booking Darth Vader with a Stormtrooper or a Jedi is a popular combo for Star Wars parties. When you hire multiple characters, they interact and create an epic galactic battle experience for the kids. We also offer Darth Vader paired with Kylo Ren for a Dark Side duo. The second character is discounted when booked together.

What does a typical Darth Vader visit include?

A typical 1-hour Darth Vader visit includes: a dramatic Imperial March entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, lightsaber training session, Force power games, Jedi vs Sith battles, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, a galactic adventure story, and additional activities.

Is your Darth Vader performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Darth Vader come for parties?

Darth Vader can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
