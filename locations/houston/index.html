Houston FAQ

Houston Party Character Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character in Houston?

Character party packages in Houston start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available. Pricing includes travel within Greater Houston. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due on the day of the event.

What areas in Houston do you serve?

We serve all of Greater Houston including Houston, Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Katy, Pearland, Cypress, Spring, League City, Missouri City, Humble, Kingwood, Richmond, and all surrounding areas.

Do you handle the Houston heat for outdoor parties?

We recommend indoor or covered venues during Houston summers for the comfort of our performers and guests. If your party is outdoors, please have a shaded area and water available. Many Houston families choose morning parties during summer months.

How far in advance should I book in Houston?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for Houston parties, especially for weekend events and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available - call us to check.

Do you serve The Woodlands and Conroe area?

Yes! We serve The Woodlands, Spring, Conroe, Magnolia, Tomball, and all of the North Houston area. Our characters are popular for birthday parties at homes, parks, and venues throughout Montgomery County.

What is your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be held as credit for up to 1 year toward a future booking. If a performer is unavailable due to illness or emergency, we'll provide a qualified substitute or offer a full refund.
