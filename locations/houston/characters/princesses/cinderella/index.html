About Our Cinderella

The Ultimate Classic Princess Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Cinderella from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the beloved fairy tale princess to life. Our Cinderella entertainers are actresses and singers who have studied the classic story extensively, perfecting Cinderella's grace, kindness, and regal bearing.

Every detail matters when creating authentic princess party entertainment. Our Cinderella costume is a premium, screen-accurate recreation of the iconic blue ball gown, complete with sparkling details, elegant white gloves, the signature choker necklace, and the classic blonde updo with headband that children recognize and love. The costume photographs beautifully, ensuring your party pictures look truly magical.

What sets our Cinderella apart is the fairy godmother magic she brings to every event. From the enchanting glass slipper ceremony to teaching royal etiquette and waltzing with the children, our Cinderella creates a genuine fairy tale experience. She also sings "A Dream is a Wish Your Heart Makes," "So This Is Love," and other beloved songs live—watching children's faces light up is pure magic.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Cinderella

How much does it cost to hire Cinderella for a birthday party?

Cinderella party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

Does your Cinderella actually sing live?

Yes! Unlike many princess performers who only play recorded music, our Cinderella entertainers are trained vocalists who sing live at every event. Your guests will hear "A Dream is a Wish Your Heart Makes," "So This Is Love," "Bibbidi-Bobbidi-Boo," and other beloved Cinderella songs performed in person by the princess herself. It's always a highlight of the party!

What ages is Cinderella best for?

Our Cinderella character is perfect for children ages 2-10, with the sweet spot being 3-7 year olds who love classic fairy tales. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from toddlers who might be a little shy to older kids who want to learn ball dancing and royal etiquette. The timeless appeal of Cinderella's story resonates across generations!

How far in advance should I book Cinderella for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Cinderella is one of our most beloved classic princess characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Cinderella with Prince Charming?

Absolutely! Booking Cinderella and Prince Charming together creates a complete fairy tale experience. When you hire both characters, they interact as the loving couple from the story, dance together, and create twice the magic. The Prince Charming add-on is discounted when booked with Cinderella. We also offer Cinderella paired with other classic princesses like Aurora, Belle, or Snow White for a "royal ball" theme.

What does Cinderella do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Cinderella visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, live singing performances, the magical glass slipper ceremony, royal ball dancing and etiquette lessons, wishing upon a star activity, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities, storytime, and additional princess games.

Is your Cinderella performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Cinderella come for parties?

Cinderella can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
