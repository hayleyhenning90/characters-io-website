Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a superhero party?
We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance. Superhero parties are especially popular for spring and summer birthdays, so book early during those seasons.
What age range is best for superhero parties?
Superhero parties work great for ages 3-12. Our performers tailor the activities based on the age group, with more active games for older kids and gentler play for younger children.
Can the superhero do flips or stunts?
While our performers are athletic and can do impressive poses and active games, for safety reasons we don't perform acrobatic stunts at parties. The focus is on interactive play and creating magical moments.
Do you have both Marvel and DC superheroes?
Yes! We have characters inspired by both Marvel and DC universes, including Spider-Man, Batman, Wonder Woman, Captain America, and many more.
Can we have a superhero and princess at the same party?
Absolutely! Combo parties are a great option for siblings or mixed groups. Many families book Spider-Man and Elsa together for ultimate party variety.
