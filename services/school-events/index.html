School Event FAQ

Are your performers background checked?
Yes, all Characters.io performers undergo comprehensive background checks. We can provide documentation for school administration as needed.
Do you offer special pricing for schools?
Yes, we offer discounted rates for educational institutions, libraries, and non-profit organizations. Contact us for a custom quote.
Can characters visit multiple classrooms?
Absolutely! We can arrange extended visits where characters move between classrooms or grade levels throughout the day.
What ages work best for character visits?
Our programs work wonderfully for Pre-K through 5th grade. We adjust the content and interaction style to be age-appropriate for each group.
Can the character read a specific book?
Yes! Characters can read from your curriculum or a book you provide. We also have a library of appropriate books featuring popular characters.
