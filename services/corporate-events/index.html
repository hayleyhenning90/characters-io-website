Corporate Event FAQ

Do you have liability insurance for corporate events?
Yes, we carry comprehensive general liability insurance. We can provide certificates of insurance (COI) as needed for your venue or corporate requirements.
Can characters be at our event for multiple hours or days?
Absolutely. We offer extended booking options for trade shows, festivals, and multi-day events. Contact us for custom pricing on extended engagements.
Do you offer volume discounts for multiple characters?
Yes, we offer package pricing when booking multiple characters for the same event. This is popular for large company picnics and trade show activations.
Can characters distribute promotional materials?
Yes, our performers can distribute branded materials, assist with photo opportunities, and help direct attendees to your booth or display.
What is your cancellation policy for corporate bookings?
We understand business schedules change. We offer flexible rescheduling options for corporate clients. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs.
