Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a princess party?
We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekend parties. During peak seasons (spring and summer), booking even earlier is advisable.
What age range is best for princess parties?
Princess parties are perfect for children ages 2-10. Our performers adjust their activities and interaction style based on the age of the birthday child and guests.
Can the princess travel to any location?
Yes! Our princesses can appear at your home, a park, a party venue, or any location of your choice. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service areas.
What songs does the princess know?
Each princess knows songs from their respective films and can perform them acapella or with backing tracks. Our Snow Queen sings "Let It Go," our Mermaid Princess sings "Part of Your World," and so on.
Can we book multiple princesses for one party?
Absolutely! Having two princesses creates an even more magical experience. Popular combinations include Snow Queen & Ice Princess, Cinderella & Rapunzel, or any princesses of your choice.
