Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Captain Jack Sparrow

How much does it cost to hire Captain Jack Sparrow for a birthday party?

Captain Jack Sparrow party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Captain Jack Sparrow do during treasure hunts?

Treasure hunts are one of the most popular activities! Captain Jack creates an exciting adventure where children follow maps and clues to discover hidden pirate treasure. He speaks in character, shares stories of his past treasure finds, and helps the crew work together to solve clues. The hunt ends with discovering treasure chests filled with gold coins and jewels that make perfect party favors.

What ages is Captain Jack Sparrow best for?

Our Captain Jack Sparrow character is perfect for children ages 5-12, with the sweet spot being 6-10 year olds who love adventure and pirate stories. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who might be a little shy to pre-teens who want to learn advanced sword fighting moves. The pirate adventure appeals to anyone who dreams of sailing the high seas!

How far in advance should I book Captain Jack Sparrow for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Captain Jack Sparrow is a popular character, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Captain Jack Sparrow with a Mermaid?

Absolutely! Booking Captain Jack Sparrow with a Mermaid is a magical combination that brings oceanic adventure to life. Together they create an exciting tale of pirates and sea creatures, lead ocean-themed games, and create twice the enchantment. We also offer Captain Jack paired with Peter Pan for an exciting Neverland pirate battle experience that kids absolutely love.

What does Captain Jack Sparrow do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Captain Jack Sparrow visit includes: a dramatic pirate entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child as first mate, exciting treasure hunts with maps and clues, sword fighting lessons with safe foam swords, his famous pirate dance and swagger, ship adventure games, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake celebration. For 2-hour packages, we add more games, pirate storytelling, and extended adventures.

Is your Captain Jack Sparrow performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Captain Jack Sparrow come for parties?

Captain Jack Sparrow can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. Outdoor settings with space for treasure hunts and sword fighting are especially fun! We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
