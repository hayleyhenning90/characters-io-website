Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Doc McStuffins

How much does it cost to hire Doc McStuffins for a birthday party?

Doc McStuffins party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Doc McStuffins do at parties?

Our Doc McStuffins character leads caring activities from the beloved Disney Junior show! Favorites include toy checkups with her doctor kit, singing "Time for Your Checkup," teaching kids about being gentle and kind, and lots of cuddles. Kids love becoming junior toy doctors!

What ages is Doc McStuffins best for?

Our Doc McStuffins character is perfect for children ages 2-7, with the sweet spot being preschoolers and early elementary kids who are big fans of the Disney Junior show. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging toddlers and young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Doc McStuffins for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Doc McStuffins is a beloved Disney Junior character, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can kids bring their own toys for checkups?

Absolutely! We encourage children to bring their favorite stuffed animals or toys for Doc to examine. Just like in the show, Doc will give each toy a caring checkup and make sure they're feeling their best. It's a wonderful interactive experience that kids treasure.

What does Doc McStuffins do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Doc McStuffins visit includes: a caring entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, toy checkups with her doctor kit, singing "Time for Your Checkup," gentle caring activities, plenty of hugs and cuddles, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities and games.

Is your Doc McStuffins performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Doc McStuffins come for parties?

Doc McStuffins can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
