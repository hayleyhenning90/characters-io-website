About Our Poppy

The Ultimate Trolls Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Poppy from Characters.io, you're bringing the happiest, most optimistic troll from the beloved Trolls movies to life for your child's special day. Our Poppy performer embodies the joyful, music-loving spirit that has made this pink-haired queen a favorite among children everywhere. Kids who love the movies will be thrilled to sing and dance with Poppy in real life!

Every detail of our Poppy costume is designed to delight young fans. The iconic bright pink hair, colorful outfit with flowers, and cheerful expression bring the animated character into the real world in the most authentic way possible. Children's eyes light up when they see Poppy walk through the door, ready to spread happiness and glitter!

What makes our Poppy parties special is the music and dancing. Just like in the beloved Trolls films, our Poppy character leads dance parties, singalongs to "Can't Stop the Feeling," and encourages everyone to find their happy place. From glitter time to hug time, kids get to experience all the magical moments that make Trolls so special.

Poppy has captured the hearts of children ages 2-8, making it the perfect party theme for toddlers through early elementary kids. The Trolls movies' emphasis on happiness, friendship, music, and believing in yourself resonates with children and creates parties that everyone enjoys.

Our Poppy character creates an atmosphere of pure joy and celebration. She leads the group in dance parties, encourages creative expression, sings with the kids, and makes sure every child feels the Trolls magic. The birthday child becomes the star as Poppy's special friend for the day!

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Poppy

How much does it cost to hire Poppy for a birthday party?

Poppy party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Poppy do at parties?

Our Poppy character leads fun activities from the beloved Trolls movies! Favorites include dance parties, glitter time, "Can't Stop the Feeling" singalongs, and of course, hug time every hour! Kids love experiencing the joy and music of Trolls in real life!

What ages is Poppy best for?

Our Poppy character is perfect for children ages 2-8, with the sweet spot being preschoolers and early elementary kids who are big fans of the Trolls movies. Our performer is especially skilled at engaging toddlers and young children, including those who might be a bit shy at first.

How far in advance should I book Poppy for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Poppy is a popular choice for rainbow and glitter-themed parties, and weekend dates fill up quickly. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Poppy and Branch together?

Absolutely! Booking both Trolls characters is a fantastic duo package. When you hire Poppy and Branch together, they interact as the beloved friends from the movies, lead activities together, and create twice the Trolls fun. The Branch add-on is discounted when booked with Poppy.

What does Poppy do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Poppy visit includes: an exciting entrance, special greeting for the birthday child, Trolls-themed activities like dance parties and glitter time, a "Can't Stop the Feeling" singalong, hug time, plenty of photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more games and activities.

Is your Poppy performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Poppy come for parties?

Poppy can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more.
