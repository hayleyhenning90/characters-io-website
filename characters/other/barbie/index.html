Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Barbie

How much does it cost to hire Barbie for a birthday party?

Barbie party packages start at $275 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like face painting or balloon twisting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What activities does Barbie do at parties?

Our Barbie leads exciting activities including fashion shows, dance parties, "I'm a Barbie Girl" singalongs, pink runway walks, and photo booth sessions. She inspires kids with the empowering message that "You can be anything!" and creates an atmosphere of fun, fashion, and friendship.

What ages is Barbie best for?

Our Barbie character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love fashion, dance, and fun. Our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who adore Barbie to tweens who appreciate the style and empowerment message.

How far in advance should I book Barbie for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Barbie is very popular, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends or December dates, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Barbie and Ken together?

Absolutely! Booking Barbie and Ken together is a popular duo package. When you hire both, they interact as the iconic couple from the Barbie world, creating twice the magic and fun. We also offer Barbie paired with Unicorn Princess, Fairy characters, or other popular characters for themed celebrations.

What does Barbie do during the party?

A typical 1-hour Barbie visit includes: a grand entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, fashion show where kids walk the runway, dance party with popular songs, "I'm a Barbie Girl" singalong, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities, games, and extended dance time.

Is your Barbie performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Barbie come for parties?

Barbie can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
