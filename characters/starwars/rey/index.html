About Our Rey

The Ultimate Star Wars Birthday Party Experience

When you hire Rey from Characters.io, you're getting more than a costume character—you're booking a professionally trained performer who brings the last Jedi to life. Our Rey entertainers have studied the character extensively, perfecting Rey's determined spirit, compassionate nature, and inspiring presence.

Every detail matters when creating authentic Star Wars party entertainment. Our Rey costume is a premium, movie-accurate recreation of the iconic Jedi outfit, complete with arm wraps, flowing layers, staff, and the signature three-bun hairstyle that children recognize and love. What sets our Rey apart is the immersive experience. Our performer stays in character the entire time, interacting with children as the real Rey would—kind, encouraging, and empowering. Kids' faces light up when they meet this legendary heroine in person and get to train alongside her.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiring Rey

How much does it cost to hire Rey for a birthday party?

Rey party packages start at $295 for a 1-hour appearance, with 2-hour packages available for larger celebrations. Pricing varies by location, date (weekends vs. weekdays), and any add-on services like a BB-8 companion or face painting. Peak season dates (spring and summer weekends) may have adjusted rates. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific event needs.

What does Rey do at the party?

Our Rey leads lightsaber training sessions where kids learn combat moves, guides Force awakening meditation, teaches Jedi mind tricks, runs exciting resistance missions, takes photos with all guests, leads the birthday song, and makes the birthday child feel like a true Jedi hero. The experience is fully interactive and empowering for all ages.

What ages is Rey best for?

Our Rey character is perfect for children ages 4-12, with the sweet spot being 5-10 year olds who love Star Wars. That said, our performers are skilled at engaging all ages—from younger kids who admire the brave heroine to older kids who want to learn lightsaber moves. Rey is especially popular with young girls who want to see a strong female Jedi!

How far in advance should I book Rey for my party?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for the best availability. Rey is one of our most popular Star Wars characters, and weekend dates fill up quickly—especially during spring birthday season (March-May) and summer. For holiday weekends, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible on weekdays.

Can I book Rey with other Star Wars characters?

Absolutely! Booking Rey with BB-8, Leia, or another Jedi is a popular combo for Star Wars parties. When you hire multiple characters, they interact and create an amazing Resistance adventure experience for the kids. We also offer Rey paired with other heroes for a Light Side celebration. The second character is discounted when booked together.

What does a typical Rey visit include?

A typical 1-hour Rey visit includes: an inspiring entrance in character, special greeting for the birthday child, lightsaber training session, Force awakening meditation, Jedi mind trick games, resistance missions, photo opportunities with all guests, and helping with the birthday cake and candles. For 2-hour packages, we add more activities, a galactic adventure story, and additional games.

Is your Rey performer background checked?

Yes, absolutely. Every Characters.io performer undergoes a comprehensive background check before working with us. We also carry full liability insurance for every event. Your family's safety and peace of mind is our top priority. All performers are also trained in child engagement and safety protocols.

Where can Rey come for parties?

Rey can appear at any location—your home, backyard, local park, party venue, restaurant, community center, school, or anywhere else you're hosting your celebration. We serve 26 major markets across the US including Los Angeles, New York, Miami, Chicago, and more. Some venues require advance coordination, so let us know your location when booking.
