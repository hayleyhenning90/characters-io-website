Common Questions

How much does it cost to hire a party character?

Character party packages start at $199 for the first hour, with additional time available at $50 per half hour. Pricing may vary by location and character availability.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends and popular characters like Elsa or Spider-Man. Last-minute bookings may be available.

What do the characters do at parties?

Our characters provide interactive entertainment including singing, dancing, games, face painting, balloon twisting, photo opportunities, and leading the birthday celebration.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a full money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied. We also guarantee on-time arrival for every event.
