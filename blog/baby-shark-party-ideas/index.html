Baby Shark Birthday Party Ideas: Doo Doo Doo Fun!

Create a fin-tastic celebration for your little one with ocean decorations, sing-along fun, and the whole shark family!

In This Article

  • The Baby Shark Theme
  • Ocean Decorations
  • Sing-Along Activities
  • Shark Food Ideas

Baby Shark has been a toddler phenomenon since the viral song took over the world. The catchy tune, simple dance moves, and colorful shark family make it perfect for young children's birthdays. Doo doo doo doo doo doo!

Why Baby Shark Parties Are Popular

  • Toddler obsession: Kids 1-4 LOVE Baby Shark
  • Built-in entertainment: The song is the activity!
  • Colorful theme: Bright, cheerful decorations
  • Family theme: Baby, Mommy, Daddy, Grandma, Grandpa sharks
  • Simple planning: Theme is straightforward

Ocean & Shark Decorations

Color Palette

  • Yellow (Baby Shark)
  • Pink (Mommy Shark)
  • Blue (Daddy Shark, ocean)
  • Orange (Grandma Shark)
  • Green (Grandpa Shark)

Essential Decorations

  • Shark cutouts: All the shark family members
  • Blue streamers: Ocean waves effect
  • Shark balloons: Yellow, pink, and blue
  • Ocean backdrop: Underwater scene for photos
  • Bubble decorations: Adds to underwater feel
  • Fishnet: Draped with sea creatures

Easy Shark Fins

Make shark fin party hats from paper plates! Cut a triangle, paint yellow (or any shark color), and attach to a headband. Instant Baby Shark costume for every guest!

Baby Shark Activities

Baby Shark Dance Party

This is THE activity. Play the song and do the dance! Over and over. Toddlers never get tired of it (parents might!).

Shark Fin Tag

Kids wear shark fins and play tag—when tagged, they become "baby sharks" too!

Fishing Game

Magnetic fishing game with fish and sharks to catch.

Bubble Time

Play "Baby Shark" while kids chase bubbles—the ocean bubbles!

Shark Bite Game

The popular "Shark Bite" board game, or create your own version with toy shark mouths.

Color the Shark Family

Coloring pages of all the shark family members.

Shark-Themed Food Ideas

Shark Snacks

  • Goldfish crackers: Obviously perfect!
  • Shark gummies: Available in party stores
  • Blue Jello cups: Ocean water with gummy fish
  • Shark fin Doritos: Chips look like fins!
  • Fish-shaped sandwiches: Use cookie cutter

Drinks

  • "Ocean water": Blue lemonade or punch
  • Juice boxes: With shark stickers added

Baby Shark Cake Ideas

  • Baby Shark face cake: Yellow with big eyes
  • Ocean scene: Blue waves with shark family toppers
  • Family shark cupcakes: Different color for each shark
  • Shark fin cake: Triangle fins emerging from waves

Toddler Party Tips

Baby Shark parties are typically for ages 1-3, so remember:

  • Keep it short (1.5-2 hours max)
  • Simple activities work best
  • Free play is important
  • Have the song ready to play on repeat!
  • Mascot characters work great for this age

Baby Shark Party Favors

  • Shark fin headbands
  • Goldfish crackers packets
  • Baby Shark stickers
  • Shark gummy candy
  • Bubbles (always a hit!)
  • Small shark toys

Final Thoughts

A Baby Shark party is simple, colorful, and perfect for toddlers. The song provides built-in entertainment, the decorations are bright and cheerful, and the theme makes everyone smile. When it's time to sing the song (for the hundredth time), watch the pure joy on your little one's face. Doo doo doo doo doo doo!
