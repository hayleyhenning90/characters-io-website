What's Typically Included

Professional princess packages include:

  • Professional performer trained in character entertainment
  • Movie-quality costume (these cost companies $500-$2000+ each)
  • Singing of character's signature songs
  • Interactive games and activities appropriate to the character
  • Coronation ceremony for the birthday child
  • Face painting or temporary tattoos (verify with company)
  • Photo opportunities with every guest
  • Travel within service area

Add-Ons That May Cost Extra

  • Extended travel outside service area
  • Balloon twisting
  • Second character
  • Specific add-on entertainment

Factors Affecting Princess Party Prices

1. Location

Prices vary by city. Metropolitan areas like Los Angeles, New York, and Miami typically cost more than smaller markets.

2. Day and Time

  • Saturdays are most expensive (highest demand)
  • Sundays slightly less expensive
  • Weekdays often have lower rates or availability specials

3. Season

  • Peak season (spring/summer birthday season): Higher prices, book early
  • Off-peak (January-February, November): Sometimes discounted

4. Character Popularity

Elsa commands premium pricing because she's consistently the most requested. Less common characters may have standard pricing.

5. Company Quality

Prices reflect costume quality and performer training. Cheaper options often mean cheaper costumes that break the illusion.

How to Save Money on Princess Parties

Book Weekdays

If your child's birthday falls on a weekday, consider having the party then. Many companies offer discounts, and performers have more availability.

Book Early

Last-minute bookings may have rush fees. Booking 3-4 weeks ahead ensures best pricing and availability.

Choose Standard Characters

Classic princesses like Cinderella and Snow White often cost less than trending characters like Elsa.

Optimize Duration

One hour is plenty for most parties under 15 kids. Don't over-book time you won't use.

Skip Add-Ons

Standard packages include plenty of entertainment. Skip balloon twisting or other add-ons if budget is tight.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to hire Elsa from Frozen?

Elsa typically costs $199-$299 for one hour, depending on location. She's our most popular character, so booking early is recommended. Elsa and Anna together is $350-$500 for one hour.

Is there a deposit required?

Most companies require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the remainder due on party day. At Characters.io, we offer flexible payment options.

Do you offer refunds if I cancel?

Policies vary by company. We offer full refunds for cancellations made 7+ days before the party. Closer cancellations may receive partial refunds or rescheduling options.

Should I tip the princess performer?

Tips are not required but always appreciated for exceptional service. $20-$50 is typical if you felt the performer went above and beyond.
