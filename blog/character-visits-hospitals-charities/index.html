Some of our most meaningful work happens outside birthday parties. Character visits to hospitals, charities, and special needs organizations bring genuine joy to children facing difficult circumstances. These visits remind us why this work matters.

The Impact of Character Visits

What We've Seen

Character visits create real moments of joy:

  • Children forget their medical situation, even briefly
  • Families experience a moment of normalcy
  • Healthcare workers see their patients light up
  • Lasting positive memories are created

Why It Matters

For children in hospitals or facing challenges, meeting their favorite character can be:

  • A bright spot in a difficult time
  • A dream they thought couldn't come true
  • A moment of pure childhood joy
  • A memory that sustains them through treatment

Sensitive Situations

Our performers are trained to handle sensitive environments with care. We work with your organization to understand specific needs, protocols, and any accommodations required.

How Hospital & Charity Visits Work

Before the Visit

  • Coordinate with hospital/organization staff
  • Review any health and safety protocols
  • Understand specific needs (mask requirements, isolation rooms, etc.)
  • Plan the visit route and timing

During the Visit

  • Character arrives in full costume
  • Staff guides character through appropriate areas
  • Individual interactions with patients/children
  • Photo opportunities (with permission)
  • Gentle, patient-paced interactions

Special Considerations

  • Infection control protocols followed
  • Costume cleaning between sensitive visits
  • Respecting patient privacy
  • Working around medical equipment/schedules

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer discounts for charities?

Yes, we offer special nonprofit rates for verified charitable organizations. Contact us to discuss your organization and budget. We try to work with nonprofits to make visits possible.

Can characters visit hospital rooms?

Yes, with hospital approval and following all infection control protocols. We work closely with hospital staff to ensure safe, appropriate visits that comply with all requirements.

Are your performers trained for sensitive situations?

Yes. Performers who do hospital and charity visits are selected for their sensitivity, patience, and ability to handle emotional situations with care.
