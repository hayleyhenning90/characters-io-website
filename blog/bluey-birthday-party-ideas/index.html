Bluey Birthday Party Ideas: Complete Heeler Family Celebration Guide

Create a Wackadoo celebration with games straight from the show, Bluey decorations, and everyone's favorite Blue Heeler!

In This Article

  • Why Kids Love Bluey
  • Bluey Decorations
  • Games from the Show
  • Bluey Food Ideas

Bluey has become one of the most beloved children's shows in recent years, and Bluey birthday parties are now among our most requested celebrations. The show's focus on imaginative play makes it perfect for party activities.

This guide covers how to throw a Bluey party with games inspired directly by the show, plus bringing Bluey herself to your celebration.

Why Bluey Parties Are Perfect for Young Kids

  • Relatable: Kids see themselves in Bluey and Bingo's adventures
  • Imaginative play focused: The show's games translate perfectly to party activities
  • Parent-approved: Parents love Bluey too (admit it!)
  • Age-appropriate: Perfect for 2-6 year olds
  • Huggable character: Mascot Bluey is perfect for hugs and photos

Bluey Party Decorations

Color Palette

  • Blue (Bluey - primary)
  • Orange/coral (Bingo)
  • Gray-blue (Bandit)
  • Reddish-brown (Chilli)
  • Bright accent colors

Essential Decorations

  • Blue and orange balloons: Representing Bluey and Bingo
  • Heeler family cutouts: All four family members
  • Paw prints: Blue paw print trail on floors/walls
  • Australian touches: Eucalyptus leaves, beach elements
  • "Wackadoo!" banner: Bluey's catchphrase
  • Backyard theme: Since many episodes happen in the backyard

The Heeler House

Create a photo backdrop that looks like the Heeler family's iconic house or backyard. Use cardboard to create simple house shapes painted in the show's colors.

Outdoor is Best

Bluey parties work wonderfully outdoors! Many of the show's games happen in the backyard, and kids have room to run around for active play.

Bluey Games (Straight from the Show!)

The best part of a Bluey party is playing the games that Bluey and Bingo play on the show. Kids will recognize them immediately!

Keepy Uppy

From the episode of the same name—keep a balloon in the air without letting it touch the ground. The whole group works together!

The Floor is Lava

A classic that Bluey and Bingo play! Scatter pillows, mats, or carpet squares as "safe spots" and call out "The floor is lava!"

Magic Xylophone

One child has the "magic xylophone" (any toy instrument). When they play it, everyone freezes. They can unfreeze people by playing again.

Shadowlands

Go outside on a sunny day and play in the shadows. Can only step on shadows!

Duck Cake

Musical chairs, but with the famous duck cake! Pass a (plastic) duck cake around while music plays.

Grannies

Kids pretend to be slow-moving grannies (hilarious for adults to watch).

Obstacle Course "Adventure"

Set up an obstacle course and pretend you're going on a Bluey adventure through the jungle, mountains, etc.

Bluey Party Food

Snacks

  • Bluey and Bingo fruit cups: Blueberries (Bluey) and orange segments (Bingo)
  • Paw print cookies: Sugar cookies with paw prints
  • Fairy bread: Australian classic that appears in the show! Butter + sprinkles on white bread
  • Veggie garden: Veggie tray (the Heelers have a garden)
  • Puppy chow: The snack mix—fits the dog theme!

Drinks

  • "Bluey Blue" lemonade: Blue-colored lemonade
  • "Bingo Orange" punch: Orange juice or orange soda
  • Water with paw print ice cubes: Freeze water in paw molds

The Duck Cake!

If you've seen the episode, you know about the famous duck cake! Try to recreate Bandit's hilariously lopsided duck cake, or order a proper Bluey-themed cake from a bakery.

Hiring a Bluey Character

A Bluey mascot character is perfect for young children because:

  • Huggable: Mascot-style characters are soft and cuddly
  • Less intimidating: Younger kids often prefer mascots to face characters
  • Playful interaction: Bluey plays games WITH the kids
  • Photo-friendly: Great for group photos and individual shots

What Bluey Does at Parties

  • Grand entrance (excited to meet everyone!)
  • Hugs and high-fives for all kids
  • Leads Bluey games like Keepy Uppy and Magic Xylophone
  • Dance party to Bluey music
  • Photo opportunities
  • Helps with cake time
