Encanto & Mirabel Birthday Party Ideas: A Magical Casita Celebration

We don't talk about Bruno, but we DO talk about planning the most magical Encanto party with Madrigal family fun and Colombian-inspired celebration!

In This Article

  • The Encanto Theme
  • Casita Decorations
  • Madrigal Activities
  • Colombian Food Ideas
  • Hiring Mirabel

Encanto captured hearts worldwide with its celebration of family, acceptance, and finding your own special gift. Mirabel's story resonates deeply with children—especially those who sometimes feel different or overlooked. The Colombian setting, magical Casita, and unforgettable music make for an incredibly vibrant party theme.

This guide covers everything for your Madrigal family celebration—plus tips on incorporating the themes of family and self-acceptance that make Encanto so special.

Why Encanto Parties Are So Popular

  • Incredible music: The songs are party-ready and kids know every word
  • Rich visuals: Colombian-inspired colors and patterns are gorgeous
  • Powerful message: Celebrates being special just as you are
  • Family focus: The Madrigal family theme works for all ages
  • Diverse characters: Many kids see themselves in Mirabel
  • Creative possibilities: Each family member's gift offers activity ideas

Casita & Colombian Decorations

Color Palette

  • Teal/turquoise (Mirabel's dress - primary)
  • Yellow/gold (Casita glow, magical candle)
  • Pink and magenta (Isabela's flowers)
  • Green (tropical plants, Bruno's vision tablets)
  • Purple (accent color throughout)
  • Butterfly colors (all colors - the iconic butterflies)

Essential Decorations

  • Paper butterflies: THE iconic Encanto element—hang everywhere!
  • The magical candle: Create a centerpiece with a golden candle display
  • Tropical flowers: Bright, colorful flower arrangements
  • Madrigal family doors: Create mini doors for each family member's room
  • Colombian patterns: Colorful textiles and patterns
  • Greenery: Plants and vines (Isabela's gift!)
  • Paper marigolds: Mexican/Colombian-inspired paper flowers

Creating the Casita

  • Use colorful tablecloths and draping
  • Hang string lights for magical glow
  • Create a "miracle" photo backdrop with the candle and butterflies
  • Display family photos (or character photos) to represent the Madrigal family
  • Add moving elements (streamers, mobiles) to represent the magical house

DIY Miracle Candle

Create the iconic candle using a large pillar candle (battery-operated for safety), gold spray paint, and a decorative holder. Add butterfly decorations around it for the magical glow effect!

Madrigal Family Gift Activities

Each family member's magical gift offers inspiration for party activities!

Discover Your Gift Station

Set up a "gift ceremony" where each child opens a door and receives a card describing their special gift (kindness, creativity, strength, etc.). Emphasize that everyone has gifts, just like Mirabel!

Isabela's Flower Crafts

Channel Isabela's flower power:

  • Make paper flowers
  • Create flower crowns
  • Paint colorful flower pictures
  • Decorate flower pots to take home

Luisa's Strength Games

Inspired by Luisa's super strength:

  • Relay races
  • Balloon "heavy lifting" (keep balloons in the air)
  • Tug of war
  • Obstacle course

Bruno's Future Telling

A fortune-telling activity (keeping it fun and positive!):

  • Magic 8-ball style predictions
  • Fortune cookies with positive messages
  • Create vision "tablets" with drawings of future dreams

Dolores' Listening Game

Inspired by Dolores' super hearing:

  • Sound guessing game (what made that noise?)
  • Telephone/whisper game
  • Musical freeze dance

Antonio's Animal Friends

For the youngest guests:

  • Stuffed animal station
  • Animal sounds guessing game
  • Make animal masks

Encanto Dance Party

You MUST play the music! Create a dance party with:

  • "We Don't Talk About Bruno"
  • "Surface Pressure"
  • "What Else Can I Do?"
  • "The Family Madrigal"

Colombian-Inspired Food Ideas

Themed Food

  • Arepas: Colombian corn cakes (easy to make or buy)
  • Empanadas: Mini versions for kids
  • Fresh tropical fruit: Mango, pineapple, papaya—Colombian flavors
  • Buñuelos: Colombian cheese balls (if you're ambitious!)
  • Mirabel's cookies: Butterfly-shaped sugar cookies
  • Colorful veggie platter: Arranged in a rainbow

Fun Themed Treats

  • Isabela's flower cupcakes: Bright pink and purple frosting flowers
  • Bruno's vision tablets: Green rice krispie treats shaped like tablets
  • Luisa's strength bars: Granola or protein bars
  • Butterfly pretzels: Pretzel sticks decorated as butterflies

Drinks

  • Tropical fruit punch: Mango, pineapple, passion fruit flavors
  • Colombian hot chocolate: (if weather appropriate)
  • "Magic candle" lemonade: Yellow lemonade with edible glitter

Encanto Cake Ideas

  • Casita cake: The magical house with glowing candle
  • Butterfly cake: Decorated with colorful butterflies
  • Mirabel-themed: Teal with butterflies and flowers
  • Family gift doors: Each tier as a different family member's door
  • Tropical flowers: Bright, Colombian-inspired floral design

Hiring a Mirabel Character

A Mirabel character brings the heart of Encanto to your celebration.

What Mirabel Does at Parties

  • Arrives in her signature teal embroidered dress and glasses
  • Tells the story of the Madrigal family
  • Leads the "discover your gift" activity
  • Sings beloved Encanto songs
  • Leads dance party to the soundtrack
  • Takes photos with each guest
  • Reminds children everyone is special (the beautiful message)

Why Children Love Mirabel

Mirabel is unique among Disney heroines—she doesn't have a magical power, yet she saves her family through love, persistence, and being herself. Children who sometimes feel left out or different connect deeply with her story.

The Message

An Encanto party celebrates:

  • Being special just as you are
  • The importance of family
  • Accepting yourself and others
  • That everyone has unique gifts to offer

We Don't Talk About Bruno (But We DO Play It)

The song is THE party anthem. Ideas for incorporating it:

  • Freeze dance (freeze when Bruno's name is said)
  • Lip sync contest
  • Group performance with simple choreography
  • Bruno costume/accessory station (hood, green glow sticks)

Encanto Party Favors

  • Butterfly temporary tattoos and stickers
  • Small succulent plants (Isabela's flowers)
  • Colorful Colombian-inspired bags
  • Encanto coloring pages and crayons
  • Small candle-shaped treat bags
  • Flower crowns (made at the party)
  • Mirabel-style glasses (frames without lenses)
  • Tropical candy

Final Thoughts

An Encanto party celebrates family, self-acceptance, and the magic of being exactly who you are. The vibrant Colombian-inspired decorations create a stunning celebration, the activities tied to each family member's gift keep kids engaged, and the incredible music makes for an unforgettable dance party. When Mirabel herself arrives, she reinforces the beautiful message that everyone has something special to offer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mirabel a Disney Princess?

While Mirabel isn't officially part of the Disney Princess lineup, she's the heroine of her story and is often included in princess parties. She resonates deeply with children and makes a wonderful party character.

What age is best for an Encanto party?

Encanto parties work wonderfully for ages 3-10. The music appeals to even older kids (and parents!), and the family themes resonate with all ages.

Can Mirabel sing all the songs?

Our Mirabel performers know the popular Encanto songs and can lead sing-alongs. They typically know "The Family Madrigal," "Waiting on a Miracle," and can lead group versions of "We Don't Talk About Bruno."
