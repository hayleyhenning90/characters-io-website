Outdoor Party Ideas (LA Weather FTW)

Los Angeles' year-round sunshine makes outdoor parties a popular choice. Options include:

Parks with Party Areas

  • Griffith Park - Multiple picnic areas, iconic location
  • Will Rogers State Park - Beautiful scenery
  • Kenneth Hahn State Recreation Area
  • Roxbury Park (Beverly Hills)
  • Local neighborhood parks (check permit requirements)

Beach Parties

  • Santa Monica Beach
  • Manhattan Beach
  • Zuma Beach (Malibu)

Note: Moana parties are perfect for beach settings!

Backyard Parties

LA backyards (when you have one!) are perfect for character entertainment. Add a bounce house and you've got a complete celebration without venue fees.

Park Permit Tip

Most LA parks require permits for parties over 25 people or with structures (bounce houses, tents). Apply 2-3 weeks ahead through LA Parks & Recreation.

LA-Specific Party Planning Tips

Weather Considerations

  • Summer: Consider afternoon parties to avoid midday heat
  • May Gray/June Gloom: Morning clouds but usually clears by noon
  • Always have shade: Even pleasant days can be too sunny
  • Mascot considerations: Mascot characters need shade and breaks in heat

Traffic Planning

  • Avoid 4-7pm party times if guests travel freeways
  • Weekend traffic can be unpredictable too
  • Give character performers generous arrival windows
  • Consider location accessibility for all guests

LA Party Trends

  • Instagram-worthy backdrops and decorations
  • Eco-friendly/sustainable party supplies
  • Food trucks for larger parties
  • Professional photographers common
  • Elaborate DIY decorations from Pinterest
