Summer Pool Party Characters: Making a Splash!

Keep the party going poolside with character entertainment that works perfectly for summer celebrations.

Pool parties are summer birthday favorites—and yes, you can absolutely have character entertainment at a pool party! While our performers don't go in the water, they create amazing poolside experiences that kids love.

Best Characters for Pool Parties

Ocean-Themed Characters

These characters fit the water theme perfectly:

  • Moana: Ocean princess—THE perfect pool party character! See our Moana party guide
  • Ariel: The Little Mermaid at a pool? Perfect match! Ariel party guide

Any Character Works!

Don't limit yourself to ocean themes—any character can do pool parties:

  • Princesses: Elsa's ice theme is perfect for hot days!
  • Superheroes: Spider-Man and superheroes are always hits
  • Mascots: Bluey loves outdoor play!

Characters Don't Swim

Our performers stay poolside—they don't go in the water. But they can interact with kids in the pool, play poolside games, lead activities on the deck, and create magical moments all around the pool area.

How Pool Party Character Visits Work

What Characters Do at Pool Parties

  • Greet guests as they arrive
  • Lead poolside games and activities
  • Interact with kids in the pool from the deck
  • Dance parties on the pool deck
  • Photo opportunities (dry areas)
  • Cake time participation
  • Story time in shaded areas

Set Up for Success

  • Shaded area: Essential for character breaks and activities
  • Indoor AC access: For mascot character breaks
  • Dry activity space: Where kids can interact with characters
  • Water for performer: Keep them hydrated!

Beat the Heat: Summer Character Tips

Timing Matters

  • Best times: Morning (before 11am) or evening (after 5pm)
  • Avoid: Peak heat hours (12pm-4pm)
  • Character duration: Shorter visits work better in heat

Mascot Characters in Summer

Mascot characters (like Bluey) require extra care in heat:

  • More frequent breaks needed
  • AC access is essential
  • Consider face characters instead for very hot days
  • We may decline extremely hot outdoor bookings for safety

What You Should Provide

  • Shaded rest area
  • Access to air conditioning
  • Cold water/drinks
  • Fan access if possible

Pool Party Activities with Characters

Poolside Games

  • Marco Polo with character cheering: Character calls out encouragement
  • Diving contests: Character judges
  • Pool noodle relay races: Character starts the race
  • Water balloon games: Character can participate!

Deck Activities

  • Dance party: Characters lead dancing on the deck
  • Story time: In shaded area
  • Craft station: Under a covered area
  • Photo ops: Before kids get wet!

Pro Tips

  • Schedule character during "dry time" (cake, activities)
  • Do photos FIRST before swimming
  • Have towels ready for kids wanting character photos mid-party

Pool Party Safety Reminder

While our performers add fun, parents should always:

  • Maintain proper supervision of swimmers
  • Have lifeguards for large parties
  • Establish pool rules before swimming starts
  • Keep rescue equipment accessible

Our performers are entertainment, not lifeguards or supervisors.

Final Thoughts

Pool parties with character entertainment create amazing summer memories! While our performers stay dry, they bring all the magic of a character appearance to your poolside celebration. Ocean princesses like Moana and Ariel are natural fits, but any character can make a splash at your pool party.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do characters go in the pool?

No, our performers stay on the pool deck for costume and safety reasons. But they can interact with kids in the pool from the side, lead poolside activities, and participate in all the dry parts of your party.

Can mascot characters work pool parties in summer?

Yes, but with precautions. We require shaded break areas and AC access. In extreme heat, we may recommend face characters instead or limit appearance times. Safety is our priority.
