Backyard Parties

Houston homes often have generous backyards perfect for parties. Add a bounce house, characters, and you're set!

Texas Heat Alert!

Houston summers are brutal. For outdoor parties May-September, schedule morning (before 11am) or evening (after 5pm). Always have shade and plenty of water. Mascot characters need extra breaks and cooling.

Houston-Specific Party Planning Tips

Weather Considerations

  • Summer (May-Sept): Extremely hot and humid—indoor or shaded venues essential
  • Hurricane season: Always have backup plan June-November
  • Spring/Fall: Perfect outdoor party weather
  • Winter: Usually mild, occasional cold fronts
  • Mascots in summer: Need frequent AC breaks—plan accordingly

Houston Traffic

  • Houston traffic is unpredictable—pad arrival times
  • I-10, 610 Loop, and 290 can be challenging
  • Give character performers generous travel windows
  • Weekend traffic is better but still busy

Houston Party Culture

  • Texans go BIG—don't be afraid to dream large
  • Diverse population means varied celebration styles
  • BBQ and Tex-Mex are popular party food
  • Space/NASA themes are uniquely Houston
  • Sports themes (Texans, Astros, Rockets) popular
