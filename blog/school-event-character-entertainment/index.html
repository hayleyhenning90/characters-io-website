Transform school events with character appearances that excite students and create lasting memories.

Best Characters for Schools

Universal Appeal (All Ages)

  • Spider-Man: Beloved by all grade levels
  • Elsa: Appeals across ages
  • Bluey: Great for younger grades (Pre-K through 2nd)
  • Mickey/Minnie: Universal recognition

Educational Tie-Ins

  • Belle: Reading programs (she's a book lover!)
  • Superheroes: Anti-bullying messages, courage
  • Moana: Environmental/ocean themes

For Large Events

Consider multiple characters for school-wide events:

  • Mix princess + superhero for broad appeal
  • 2-3 characters for 300+ students
  • Characters can rotate through grade levels

Age-Appropriate Selection

Elementary schools: Most characters work great. Middle school: Superheroes tend to work best. High school: Consider other entertainment options unless specifically requested.

Planning Tips for Schools

Logistics

  • Designate a coordinator: One person manages character arrival, schedule
  • Private changing area: Characters need a space away from students
  • Break room: Especially for outdoor/long events
  • Clear schedule: Which grades when, photo logistics

Managing Large Groups

  • Schedule by grade level or classroom
  • Rotate characters through cafeteria during lunch
  • Set up photo stations with crowd management
  • Have teachers help manage lines

Communication

  • Get administrator approval first
  • Check for allergies (for any food-related activities)
  • Inform parents (some may want to attend)
  • Brief teachers on the schedule
