The Easter Bunny is a springtime icon! Whether you're hosting a neighborhood egg hunt, church celebration, or spring birthday party, the Easter Bunny brings magic to any Easter event.

Types of Easter Events

Egg Hunts

The classic Easter activity! The Easter Bunny can:

  • Welcome families as they arrive
  • Start the egg hunt countdown
  • Take photos with children
  • Distribute special prizes

Community/Church Events

  • Large-scale egg hunts
  • Easter festivals
  • Photos with the Easter Bunny
  • Spring celebrations

Home Easter Parties

  • Family gatherings with kids
  • Backyard egg hunts
  • Neighborhood parties

Spring Birthday Parties

  • April birthdays with Easter themes
  • Spring-themed celebrations
  • Bunny-themed parties any time

Corporate Spring Events

  • Company family days
  • Office Easter celebrations
  • Employee appreciation events

What the Easter Bunny Does

At Events

  • Mascot-style character (soft, huggable)
  • Photos with children and families
  • Roaming and interacting with guests
  • Can help start egg hunts
  • Distribute baskets or prizes (you provide)

Photo Opportunities

Professional Easter Bunny photos are a highlight:

  • Set up a photo station with spring backdrop
  • Have photographer ready (or self-serve camera)
  • Easter props (baskets, eggs, flowers)

Book Early for Easter!

Easter Sunday and the weekend before are our busiest spring dates. Book 4-6 weeks ahead for best availability. Like Santa, popular Easter dates fill up fast!

Easter Party Activities

Egg Hunt

The main event! Tips:

  • Separate areas for different ages
  • Include special "golden eggs" with prizes
  • Have extra eggs ready
  • Set clear boundaries

Egg Decorating

  • Pre-dyed eggs for stickers and decorations
  • Plastic eggs to decorate
  • Easter craft stations

Bunny Hop Games

  • Bunny hop races
  • Musical "nests" (like musical chairs)
  • Egg and spoon races
  • Pin the tail on the bunny

Photos with the Easter Bunny

Schedule photo sessions throughout the event to manage lines.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far ahead should I book for Easter?

Book at least 4-6 weeks ahead for Easter events. Easter Sunday and the weekend before are extremely busy—these dates often book out by early March.

Does the Easter Bunny bring eggs/baskets?

No, you provide eggs, baskets, and any giveaway items. The Easter Bunny can help distribute them and interact with children, but doesn't bring the hunt supplies.
